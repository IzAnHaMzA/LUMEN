I. RATIONALE

Computer hardware system course focuses on the skill development toward the configuration and maintenance of computer systems and its peripherals. The course will enable students to maintain computer system using various software and hardware diagnostic tools.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Maintain the Computer Systems.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify Different Component of Motherboards.
  • CO2 - Troubleshoot Power Supply.
  • CO3 - Troubleshoot Peripherals Components of Portable Devices.
  • CO4 - Troubleshoot Input/output and Storage Device.
  • CO5 - Find Tune Performance of given Computing Systems.
