I. RATIONALE

A large number of mining machineries are used in the mine right from the winding of men and material through shafts, transport of material, wire, power for drilling, cutting and loading of coal on the faces. Pumping operations are also essential to deal with accumulation of water in underground workings. A mining engineer should be aware of the types of Power cables used for different machines , so that they can supervise the selection, installation and day-to-day operation and elementary maintenance of these equipment.
