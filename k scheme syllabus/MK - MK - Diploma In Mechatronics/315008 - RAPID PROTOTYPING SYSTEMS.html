I. RATIONALE

Rapid prototyping methods are vital in modern engineering, manufacturing, and design because they allow ideas to be quickly transformed into functioning prototypes. This topic teaches diploma students skills such as solid modeling and 3D printing of cost-effective components. It prepares students to flourish in industrial situations by encouraging creativity, efficiency and practical problem-solving skills.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply Rapid Prototyping systems to produce cost-effective 3D printed components.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop a 3D solid model for a given drawing.
  • CO2 - Select a relevant rapid prototyping process.
  • CO3 - Select relevant material for manufacturing of prototype.
  • CO4 - Develop the given prototype by using the FDM 3D printing process.
