I. RATIONALE

This course aims to develop students' skills in the maintenance of a wide range of electrical and electronic equipment, appliances, and gadgets used in both industrial applications and everyday life. Students will learn to use modern tools for analyzing, designing, and performing real-time testing of analog as well as digital circuits

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Maintain Electrical and Electronic equipment's by using various tools.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify various tools to maintain given electrical appliances.
  • CO2 - Maintain different electrical appliances safely.
  • CO3 - Select troubleshooting tools for the given electronic equipment.
  • CO4 - Maintain electronic appliances and laboratory equipment.
