Course Title : SURFACE PREPARATION AND PAINT APPLICATIONS METHODS
Course Code : 325327
I. RATIONALE

This course will introduce students to various surface preparation methods, industrial paint application methods such as conventional spraying, airless/air-assisted airless spraying, robotic spraying, electrodeposition, coil coating etc. This course will also give insight to various paint shop process and equipment like spray booths, ovens, sludge collecting units, VOC management etc. This course will make students to understand paint film defects, their causes, and remedies. This course also introduces powder coating application methods with powder coating quality control.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply liquid paint and powder coating as per specification.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Utilize appropriate surface preparation methods.
  • CO2 - Apply paint using suitable paint application techniques.
  • CO3 - Explain paint shop processes and equipment.
  • CO4 - Analyze paint film defects, their causes, and remedies.
  • CO5 - Select electroplating and powder coating method.
