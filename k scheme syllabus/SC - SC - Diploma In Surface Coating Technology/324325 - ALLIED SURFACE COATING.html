I. RATIONALE

The course 'Allied Surface Coating' is included in fourth semester of Diploma in Surface Coating Technology to enable student to understand applications of surface coating principles, process and techniques in allied industries. The course will enable students to learn and practice floor coating, cosmetics, polishes, water proofing and marine coatings. This course will also help students develop an insight into developing entrepreneurship.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: Apply industrial Allied Surface Coating techniques.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Explain principles and applications of Floor Coating.
  • CO2 - Classify types of Polishes processes.
  • CO3 - Describe raw materials requirement for cosmetics.
  • CO4 - Describe waterproofing processes.
  • CO5 - Test performance properties of Marine coating materials.
