I. RATIONALE

Architectural Design is one of the key courses necessary in Architectural Education. This course enables a learner to understand the various layers that are integral part of Architectural Profession. It also enables learner to understand & attain basic skills required for architectural design course. The learner will develop the graphical skills required to express design.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
A learner shall be able to design an architectural project having an area upto 50-75 Sq. Meters
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply basic concepts of Architectural Design for the given project.
  • CO2 - Prepare Architectural Drawings for the given project.
  • CO3 - Present reports on Case Study and Site Visit undertaken during the course.
  • CO4 - Prepare drawings for the given project using CAD Software.
