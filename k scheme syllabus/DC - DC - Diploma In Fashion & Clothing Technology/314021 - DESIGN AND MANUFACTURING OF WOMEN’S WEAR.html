I. RATIONALE

Designing and producing quality garments are of tremendous importance in the fashion world. In this course students will gain insights into various designing aspects, learning to tackle real-world production issues and managing costs effectively. The students will also learn about brand positioning, design establishment, fashion cycle, and patterning skills necessary for a fashion professional.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help the student attain the industry's expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences:- Design women's wear in accordance with modern trends and industrial standards.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Design various apparel for women’s wear depending on the end uses.
  • CO2 - Develop the garment component layout/marker plan to optimize fabric consumption.
  • CO3 - Develop various women’s wear using various machines.
  • CO4 - Apply the various finishing techniques according to quality specifications.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Visit
  • Visit a fashion boutique to collect pictures of garment designing and manufacturing processes.
  • Visit to garment industry and submit a report on garment production process flow.
  • Visit the garment manufacturing industry to collect data regarding various quality checkpoints used for women’s wear.
    • Micro project
  • Design a sustainable casual wear collection for women, emphasizing eco-friendly materials and processes.
  • Analyze key features of any famous designer's collection and collect photos or sketches related to the type of material, embellishment, style, and color. Design two garments based on the designer's collection.
  • Prepare operation bulletins for different garments.
  • Select any one well-known women’s wear brand and collect their special features in the form of photos or sketches related to the type of material, embellishment, style, and color. Design two garments based on a brand study.
    • Assignment
  • Collect the pictures of recent national fashion weeks for formal, ethnic wear, fusion wear, party wear, casual wear, and innerwear and prepare the catalog for the same.
  • Collect the pictures of Indo-western garments and prepare the catalog for the same.
  • Outline the stages of quality control from fabric inspection to final garment inspection.
  • Market survey for collecting materials for developing women’s apparel.
