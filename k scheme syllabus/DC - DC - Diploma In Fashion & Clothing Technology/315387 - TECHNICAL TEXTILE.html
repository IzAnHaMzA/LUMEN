I. RATIONALE

Technical textiles have been slowly gaining ground due to functional requirement, health & safety, cost effectiveness, durability, high strength, light weight, versatility, customization, user friendliness, eco friendliness, logistical convenience etc. This course will help students to select raw material, type of manufacturing process, process parameters for production of technical textile suitable for required end use application. This course encompasses various types of raw materials used, their properties, various types of manufacturing processes used, various technical textile products and their applications.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Solve problems related to manufacturing technical textile producsts.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Choose the manufacturing process to produce a specific non-woven textile product.
  • CO2 - Select fibres, manufacturing methods, and parameters for geotextile production based on the end-use.
  • CO3 - Utilize textile fibre properties and manufacturing techniques to create medical textiles for specific applications.
  • CO4 - Choose fibres, manufacturing methods, and parameters for producing agricultural and automotive textiles.
  • CO5 - Select fibres, yarns, and fabrics for technical textiles in defense applications.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Collect photographs of various manufacturing processes of non-woven fabric production and prepare a booklet detailing these processes.
  • Collect ten different samples of various non-woven fabrics and prepare a catalog showing a swatch of each sample along with its technical features, such as the type of fiber used, manufacturing method and GSM (grams per square meter).
  • Prepare a PowerPoint presentation on geo textiles, covering the following points: introduction, global usage of geo textiles, specifications of fibers and yarns used, manufacturing methods, different functions of geo textiles and applications of geo textiles.
  • Collect five different samples of textile materials used in the medical area and prepare a Power Point presentation on their features, unique properties and manufacturing processes.
  • Prepare a PowerPoint presentation on the application of medical textiles, including non-implantable materials, extracorporeal devices, implantable materials and healthcare and hygiene products.
  • Collect and prepare catalogue of different samples of shade nets used in agriculture.
  • Prepare a PowerPoint presentation on various types of technical textiles used in automobiles, detailing their functional requirements.
  • Prepare a PowerPoint presentation on defense textiles, including information about the classification of defense textiles, specifications of fibers, yarns, and fabrics used, functional requirements and applications of defense textiles.
  • Collect at least ten samples of technical fabrics (woven/knitted/non-woven) used in different technical areas and prepare a catalog. Include comments on the type of fiber used, its specifications (if any), manufacturing method and end use.
  • Prepare a PowerPoint presentation on agro textiles, containing information on the types of fibers and yarns used, manufacturing methods and applications in various areas of agriculture.
    • Assignment
  • Draw schematic diagrams of different methods of non woven fabric production and stick it on a half imperial size card board paper.
  • Draw a pie chart showing different types of technical textiles being used.
  • Collect recent data about different types of technical textiles used /consumed in India (kg/meters/Rupees). Present the data in a pie chart or bar chart.
  • Collect data on different types of geo textiles being used by different countries world over in recent past. Present the data in the form of Pie chart or Bar chart
  • Prepare a report on different types of sutures used during surgical procedure. Collect information about different types of fibers/yarns used for production of these sutures, manufacturing process
  • Prepare a report of Airbags used in automobiles containing information on importance of airbag, different types of fibers/ filaments used for production of airbags, required functional properties of airbag how it works in case of mishap.
  • Prepare a power point presentation on Ballistic protection - principle of ballistic protection, fibres, yarns and fabrics used for ballistic protection, details of ballistic vest used by soldiers during combat operation.
