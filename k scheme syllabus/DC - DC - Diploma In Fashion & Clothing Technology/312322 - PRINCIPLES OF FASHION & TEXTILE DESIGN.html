I. RATIONALE

Textile and fashion designers should have knowledge about the basic elements and principles of fashion designing, which include repetition, alteration, harmony, gradation, balance, etc. Also, the subject deals with the origin of fashion, basic terminologies in fashion, the composition of textile designs, psychology of clothing. Hence, this course develops skills related to textile and fashion design.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences: Apply relevant elements and principles of design in the textile and fashion industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply elements and principles of design in fashion and textile.
  • CO2 - Use relevant color theories and harmonies for developing textile and fashion designs.
  • CO3 - Analyze the composition of various traditional and innovative textile designs.
  • CO4 - Analyze the psychology of clothing for various situations.
  • CO5 - Differentiate various garments on the basis of clothing and fashion terminologies.
