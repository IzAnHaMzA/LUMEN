I. RATIONALE

The Garment manufacturing process requires numerous machineries for their manufacture. Also certain super-specialized machineries are used in this field. This course introduces these machineries and uses, their assemblies & parts in detail. Student will learn the different process sequence steps in apparel industry and learn different sewing machine settings that will help the student to overcome the problems in production of garments.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences: Use various types of garment machineries & equipment for mass production.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop marker plan for various fabric widths.
  • CO2 - Use cutting machine to separate various garment components.
  • CO3 - Select various needles for various fabrics and sewing machines.
  • CO4 - Use various sewing machines for various settings.
  • CO5 - Use work-aids and attachments for various sewing machines.
  • CO6 - Use fusing and pressing equipment.
