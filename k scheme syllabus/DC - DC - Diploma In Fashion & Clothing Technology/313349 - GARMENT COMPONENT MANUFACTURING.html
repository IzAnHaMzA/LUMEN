I. RATIONALE

Development of individual components of garment is the first step towards entire garment assembly prior to its manufacturing. Modifications in garment component manufacturing plays vital role in fashion industry. The course provides knowledge to achieve expertise in manufacturing individual garment components. This course will enable the students to modify the garment components using appropriate process, and also to construct different garments components from basics. It also makes them aware of the different garment finishing and enclosure techniques.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Use relevant design principles and procedures in manufacturing different garment components.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop different seam and stitch types.
  • CO2 - Develop different modified plackets, pockets and cuffs.
  • CO3 - Implement fullness principle in garment component construction.
  • CO4 - Develop different collars, necklines and sleeves.
  • CO5 - Apply different garment finishing and enclosure techniques.
