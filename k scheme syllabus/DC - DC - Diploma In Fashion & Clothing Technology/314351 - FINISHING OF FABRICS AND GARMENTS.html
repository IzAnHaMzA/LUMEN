I. RATIONALE

Finishing is an important process to increase the function-ability and market value of the garment. Certain garment properties also increase by the applying mechanical and chemical finishing. Finishing process also increases the quality of fabric and garments. This also remove fault from the fabric. This course will facilitate students to use principles of finishing and apply them for relevant garments in the fashion industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry / employer expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences.
Use various finishing techniques for fabric and garments in the fashion industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use various machines to produce various mechanical finishes on various fabrics.
  • CO2 - Use chemical finishes on various fabrics and garments.
  • CO3 - Produce various wash down effects on relevant garments.
  • CO4 - Use relevant machines for finishing of garments.
  • CO5 - Select relevant stain removing & dry-cleaning treatments for the given garments.
