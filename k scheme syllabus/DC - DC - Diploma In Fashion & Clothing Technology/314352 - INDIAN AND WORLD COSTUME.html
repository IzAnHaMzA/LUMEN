I. RATIONALE

Designing and creativity are unique features of the fashion industry. Different regional and international costumes influence the designing of fashion garments. This course introduces information about the unique features of different Indian and world costumes. This course highlights the ethnic costume designs the world over and also the famous ethnic wear in India. It also showcases the garments and accessories of places having global importance.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry-expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Design contemporary fashion costumes based on Indian and World costumes.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Create various costumes styles inspired from the Western costumes.
  • CO2 - Create various costume styles inspired from the Eastern and Middle east costumes.
  • CO3 - Design various costume styles inspired from the North Indian costumes.
  • CO4 - Design various costume styles inspired from the South Indian costumes.
  • CO5 - Design various costume styles inspired from the East Indian costumes.
