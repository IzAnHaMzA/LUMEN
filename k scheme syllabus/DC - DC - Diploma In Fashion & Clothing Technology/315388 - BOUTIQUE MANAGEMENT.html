I. RATIONALE

Boutique start-up is the upcoming trend in fashion industry. The entrepreneurs must have necessary knowledge and skills to manage a boutique successfully. Boutique management includes fashion merchandising, store operations, customer service, marketing and customer relationship management. This course is designed to blend the theoretical concepts with practical applications to prepare students for careers in boutique management.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student attain the industry-expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Manage Boutique operations effectively by applying management principles.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop plans for necessary registrations and permissions to start a boutique.
  • CO2 - Select various ranges of merchandises and vendors for the boutique store.
  • CO3 - Select effective sales promotion techniques for various fashion products.
  • CO4 - Identify various methods for maintaining stores, accounts & customer records.
  • CO5 - Suggest strategies to create unique brand identity for various products.

  • Prepare a report to plan a social media marketing campaign to promote the new product line.
  • Create a 3D window display for a boutique based on the given theme and product.
  • Prepare a report on implementing eco-friendly practices in store / boutique operations and merchandising.

  • Prepare a catalog by collecting articles from fashion magazines and newspapers related to boutique promotion.
  • Prepare a storybook of successful boutique entrepreneurs.
  • Collect the formats of book-keeping required in boutique management.
  • Prepare a presentation showing new trends, customer preferences and the competitive landscape for upcoming boutiques.

  • Visit fashion boutique to understand store layout, customer flow and visual merchandising strategies.

  • Visit trade shows and fashion markets to identify emerging trends and new products.
