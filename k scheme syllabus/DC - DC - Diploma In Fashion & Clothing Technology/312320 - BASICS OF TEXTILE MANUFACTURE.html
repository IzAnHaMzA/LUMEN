I. RATIONALE

Diploma engineers (also called technologists) must deal with various textile materials and machines in industries. The study of basic concepts of yarn and fabric manufacturing like blow room, carding, combing, speed frame, ring frame, looms, warp, weft, etc. will help them understand the textile manufacturing processes where emphasis is laid on the applications of textile material. This course is developed in a way by which fundamental information will help diploma engineers apply the basic principles of yarn and fabric manufacturing in textile processing to solve broad-based problems

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply principles of yarn and fabric manufacturing process to produce fabric.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use pre-opening principles to deliver quality material for subsequent processes
  • CO2 - Apply the principles of spinning to produce the yarn.
  • CO3 - Use the yarn numbering system to estimate the yarn size.
  • CO4 - Select the relevant preparatory process to produce fabric
  • CO5 - Apply the principle of interlacement to produce plain woven fabric.
  • CO6 - Use the principle of design to reproduce the textile material
