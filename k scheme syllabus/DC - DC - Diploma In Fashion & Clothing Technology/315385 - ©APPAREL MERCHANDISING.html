I. RATIONALE

The role of merchandising in the fashion industry is constantly on the rise as garments are supplied in-country and are also exported to countries outside the national frontiers. Merchandisers have to act as an important link between the buyer and the manufacturer in both the domestic and the export apparel industries. Students need to be aware of different roles of merchandisers being procurement of orders, store- purchase operations, marketing strategy formulation, product and visual merchandising. This course enables the students to learn about various inventory control techniques. This subject orients the student to carry out the merchandising processes and equip themselves with the marketing skills related to apparel merchandising.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help students attain industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: Apply the principles of merchandising in marketing the fashion garments in both the domestic and global markets.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the steps in fashion marketing.
  • CO2 - Interpret the effect of macro and micro environments on fashion marketing.
  • CO3 - Select the marketing strategies for fashion products based on the life cycle stage.
  • CO4 - Prepare the operation bulletin for fashion product manufacturing.
  • CO5 - Develop a visual merchandising plan for specified fashion products.
  • CO6 - Select the inventory control techniques for fashion industry.
