I. RATIONALE

The quality of woven fabric depends on its functional and aesthetic properties. These in turn are governed by the selection of raw material, yarns used, fabric construction, structure, texture, and fabric ornamentation. The end use emphasizes the functional and aesthetical aspects, thus the fabric structure gains immense importance. In this course, students study the basics of fabric structure regarding weave, raw material, feel, weight, and appearance. The course imparts knowledge on popular weaves like plain weave, twill weave, Sateen/satin weave, and furnishing weaves.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Identify the weaves in the fabric depending on the end use.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Analyse the basic parameters of the various woven fabrics.
  • CO2 - Use various plain weave derivative fabric for the various end uses of the garment.
  • CO3 - Use various derivatives of twill weaves for various end use.
  • CO4 - Apply lustrous effect in fabric through sateen / satin weaves.
  • CO5 - Select various fancy weaves for various furnishing applications.
