I. RATIONALE

Production maximization, quality enhancement are important needs of the garment manufacturing industry. This course has been designed keeping in view these industry related needs. This course highlights on basic skills of industrial engineering tools needed for garment production. They will be able to apply the principles of industrial engineering and management for continuous improvement in the garment industry. This course will facilitate students to install and improve an integrated system of men, material and machine by using the quantitative techniques of management.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help students attaint industry identifies competency through various teaching - learning experiences: Apply principles of industrial engineering and production management to garment industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use work study to maximize effectiveness of man and machine.
  • CO2 - Use principles of planning and process control in a garment manufacturing industry.
  • CO3 - Use network analysis techniques of CPM (Critical Path Method) and PERT (Program Evaluation and Review Techniques) in project management.
  • CO4 - Evaluate production cost and Break-even quantity for given situation.
  • CO5 - Prepare maintenance activity plan for the garment manufacturing industry.
