I. RATIONALE

Pattern making plays a vital role in the garment industry. Proficiency in taking accurate body measurements and creating customer-specific patterns is vital in the fashion sector. The course aims to enhance students' skills in basic pattern making, enabling them to apply scientific sizing methods and create commercial patterns from fundamental designs.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Develop patterns through drafting techniques for diverse garments.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select effective measurement techniques and tools for pattern making.
  • CO2 - Identify the symbols, techniques and principles used in pattern making.
  • CO3 - Develop patterns for womens’ wear.
  • CO4 - Develop patterns kids wear and mens’wear.
  • CO5 - Develop given garment components for specified use.
