I. RATIONALE

The escalating global competition within the fashion realm has elevated the significance of worldwide standards in quality, waste minimization, and cost efficiency, than ever before. The basic concepts, and principles of clothing quality management are included in this course. Aspects of quality, inspection, and testing of fabrics, trims, accessories, and garments, and ongoing process control by different tools will help students in the selection of suitable fabrics, processes, accessories, and trims. It will also help in quality assurance, cost cutting, waste reduction, and customer satisfaction in garment industries.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences:
Perform quality checks on trims, fabrics, manufacturing processes, and apparels.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the check points for various quality dimensions in various garments.
  • CO2 - Inspect the fabrics, trims for various quality aspects.
  • CO3 - Inspect the quality control points in various garment manufacturing processes for checking acceptability.
  • CO4 - Evaluate various fabrics and garments on various quality standards.
  • CO5 - Prepare quality improvement plan for various garment industries.
