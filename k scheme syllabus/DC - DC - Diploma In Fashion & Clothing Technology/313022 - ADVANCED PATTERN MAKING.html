I. RATIONALE

Pattern-making is vital for garment production. The students can develop patterns by manipulating in basic patterns, drape the stylized garments, grade the basic pattern into a variety of sizes, and also the understanding of diverse body types and customer-specific patterns, empowers students to craft commercial patterns from foundational designs in the ever-evolving field of fashion.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help the student attain the industry's expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Design advanced patterns using manipulation, draping, and grading techniques.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply dart manipulation and contouring techniques to modify patterns.
  • CO2 - Develop paper patterns for kids, men, and women using pattern manipulation.
  • CO3 - Develop patterns using the draping principles.
  • CO4 - Apply grading techniques to develop patterns of different sizes.
  • CO5 - Select accurate apparel and fittings based on human figure analysis.
