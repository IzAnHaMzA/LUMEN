I. RATIONALE

Fashion drawing is the initial step in design, enabling visualization before sewing. The Fashion Drawing course aims to equip students with fundamental skills and knowledge in the art of fashion illustration. It is essential to learn human anatomy, various poses, fabric rendering, and develop a unique style. Students will be able to create compelling fashion illustrations, incorporating creativity, technical proficiency, and cultural influences.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Render fashion garments for emerging trends and creative styles.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select relevant tools and shading techniques for various drawings and sketching.
  • CO2 - Draw fashion figures, advanced poses and gestures for men, women, and kids.
  • CO3 - Render different fabrics, patterns, textures, and fabric draping elements.
  • CO4 - Render garments and accessories based on various theme and situations.

  • Drape garments on dummy in different ways and take photographs for report preparation.
  • Collect photographs of different poses of fashion models from fashion shows.
  • Compile sketches of various facial expressions of men, women and kids.
