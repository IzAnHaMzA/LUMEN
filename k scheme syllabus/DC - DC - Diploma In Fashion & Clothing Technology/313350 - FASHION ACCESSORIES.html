I. RATIONALE

Fashion accessories are items that complement or enhance the wearer’s outfit, such as shoes, bags, belts, hats, scarves, jewellery, etc. Fashion accessories are an important part of the fashion industry, as they can create new trends, add value to existing products, and generate revenue for designers and retailers. This course aims to develop the abilities of designing, making and costing of fashion accessories.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Develop various fashion accessories using different materials and techniques depending on end use.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Classify the fashion accessories for various situations.
  • CO2 - Develop designs for handbags and gloves using innovation and creativity.
  • CO3 - Develop designs for shoes and belts using creativity and innovation.
  • CO4 - Develop designs for hats and neckwear using creativity and innovation.
  • CO5 - Apply construction techniques for making various Indian jewelleries.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Collect pictures of historical and current accessories and prepare catalogs with brief details of each.
  • Collect waste materials and prepare different accessories from those materials like belt, hat, etc.
  • Collect natural materials such as flowers, leaves, wood, and shells and prepare different accessories from those materials like belt, hat, jewellery, etc.
  • Conduct research for searching history of fashion accessory such as handbag, hat, jewellery, etc.
    • Assignment
  • Classify the fashion accessories according to the situation such as bridal wear, party wear, casual wear, formal wear, etc.
  • Prepare power point presentation for showing latest trends in fashion accessories.
  • Draw ten flat sketches of female accessories.
  • Draw ten flat sketches of male accessories.
    • Visit
  • Visit to any accessory industry such as handbag industry, shoes industry, etc.
  • Visit to street fashion store to observe latest trends in accessories.
  • Visit boutiques, designer shops, and branded shops for current trends in fashion accessories.
