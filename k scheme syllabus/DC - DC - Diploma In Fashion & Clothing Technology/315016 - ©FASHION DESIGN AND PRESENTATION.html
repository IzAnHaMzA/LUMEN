I. RATIONALE

Fashion design collection and presentation is about creating and showcasing a coordinated series of garments with a unified theme. This course provides insights on students' skills in garment design through fashion forecasting, focusing on creative use of colors, patterns and fabrics. Practical sessions guide students from trend prediction to garment construction. Additionally, this course covers garment costing, enabling students to create innovative and cost-effective fashion collections.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry-expected outcome through various teaching- learning experiences: Develop distinctive fashion collections to present upcoming fashion industry trends and standards.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Analyze fashion history and key movements to forecast future trends.
  • CO2 - Develop design concepts and supporting boards through research and theme exploration.
  • CO3 - Design garments, create patterns and manage manufacturing and quality control.
  • CO4 - Prepare fashion portfolio presentations for specific client.
