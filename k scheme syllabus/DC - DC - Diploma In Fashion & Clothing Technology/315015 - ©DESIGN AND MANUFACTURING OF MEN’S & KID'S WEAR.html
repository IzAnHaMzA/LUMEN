I. RATIONALE

The growth in sectors of men’s and kids’ wear in fashion require expertise in drafting and stitching garments. The course provides insights on designing, pattern drafting and brand positioning, helping students manage production challenges and market their creations effectively. Students will use various stitching machines to ensure accuracy and perfection while learning about costing, design establishment, the fashion cycle and patterning.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry-expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Develop modern garment styles for formal wear, casual wear, sportswear and outerwear for men and kids in compliance with industrial standards.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Design various apparel for men’s wear and kids wear.
  • CO2 - Cut the garment components as per given marker plan.
  • CO3 - Develop various men’s wear and kids wear.
  • CO4 - Apply the various finishing techniques according to quality specifications.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Assignment
  • Prepare a comparative analysis report of national and international brands of men's wear and kids' wear
  • Prepare detailed operation bulletins for various kids' and men’s garments.
  • Prepare a summary of key features and insights from the party wear designer's collection.
  • Prepare a booklet of showing classification and understand the different categories of men’s wear and kids’ wear.
    • Micro project
  • Design garments inspired by the latest trends and the studied designer’s collection and prepare a mini portfolio.
  • Study key features of designer collections that incorporate the latest trends and design garments inspired by them.
  • Analyze the brand identity and specifications of selected brands.
  • Research and list the latest trends in men’s wear categories: formal wear, casual wear, sportswear and outerwear.
  • Research and list the latest trends in kids’ wear categories: formal wear, casual wear, sportswear and outerwear.
    • Visit
  • Visit a fashion boutique to collect pictures of garment designing and manufacturing processes.
  • Visit to garment industry and submit a report on garment production process flow.
  • Visit the garment manufacturing industry to collect data regarding various quality checkpoints used for men’s wear and kids' wear.
