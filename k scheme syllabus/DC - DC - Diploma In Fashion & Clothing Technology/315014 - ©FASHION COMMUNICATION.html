I. RATIONALE

Fashion communication is conveying fashion-related messages and narratives through various media channels. Its primary aim is to create a compelling story around fashion products, brands or trends, making them appealing and desirable to consumers. This field integrates elements of marketing, branding, journalism, visual arts and digital media to communicate fashion's cultural and social significance effectively. This course helps students providing insights on public perception, influence trends and drive consumer engagement in the fashion industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Apply the communication techniques for effective communication of fashion creations.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify various fashion medias to promote fashion garments.
  • CO2 - Identify various tools for promoting various fashion brand.
  • CO3 - Prepare portfolios for various fashion photography and styling.
  • CO4 - Forecast the future trends for fashion products.
