I. RATIONALE

In industry, automation in every field is being used, of which microcontroller is an inbuilt element. Knowledge and skills related with microcontrollers is very vital, since it is the heart of embedded systems and most of the high end electronic products. This course is intended to develop the skills to diagnose and rectify embedded system related problems especially in the healthcare industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry/employer expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences:
Maintain biomedical instruments based on microcontroller and embedded systems.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select the microcontroller for various applications.
  • CO2 - Use IDE to write embedded C programs for AT89C51 microcontroller.
  • CO3 - Interface I/O devices and medical sensors with microcontroller.
  • CO4 - Use various communication protocols required in medical systems.
  • CO5 - Maintain embedded systems in biomedical applications.
