I. RATIONALE

Human anatomy and physiology is a core course, which is a prerequisite of all courses related with Medical Electronics. This course will enable the student to appreciate that anatomy is the science of understanding the structure and the parts of living organisms. Physiology, on the other hand, deals with the internal mechanisms/functions and the processes that work towards sustaining life. Thus human anatomy and physiology course correlates the knowledge of anatomy and functionality of human body systems in operating medical instruments.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help students to attain the following hospital / medical industry / employer expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences:
Correlate anatomical structure and physiological functionality of various human body systems with appropriate medical instruments.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Interpret the functioning of cell, tissues and skeletal system of the body.
  • CO2 - Choose the equipment related to cardiovascular and respiratory system.
  • CO3 - Corelate the equipment with regards to the digestive and excretory system.
  • CO4 - Interpret the function of various organs and effect of hormones with regards to reproductive system.
  • CO5 - Identify the instrument related to nervous system and special senses.
