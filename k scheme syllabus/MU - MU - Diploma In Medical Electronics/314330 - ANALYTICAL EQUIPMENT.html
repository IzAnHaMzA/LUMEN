I. RATIONALE

A comprehensive understanding of modern analytical equipment used for diagnosing various physiological abnormalities is essential for medical electronics technicians. This course equips students with the necessary skills to handle state-of-the-art optical and sterilization equipment in hospitals, health care units, pathological and research laboratories

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to attend the following industry/employer expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences:
Maintain various analytical equipment.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use basic analytical equipment.
  • CO2 - Operate different types of sterilizing and radiochemical equipment.
  • CO3 - Measure the quantity of different components in blood using analytical instruments.
  • CO4 - Quantify pH and conductivity of blood fluid.
  • CO5 - Measure different pollutants present in the environment.
