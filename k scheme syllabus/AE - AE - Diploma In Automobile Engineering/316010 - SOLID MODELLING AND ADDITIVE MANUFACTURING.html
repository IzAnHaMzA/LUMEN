I. RATIONALE

The Solid Modeling and Additive Manufacturing course is designed to equip students of diploma in the automobile and allied Industries with essential skills in solid modeling techniques and additive manufacturing technologies. This course will empower students to apply solid modeling and additive manufacturing concepts and methodologies in the design, development, and production of mechanical parts.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry identified outcome through various teaching learning experiences: Apply skills to create, prototype, and manufacture automobile components using solid modeling and additive manufacturing techniques.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Prepare 2D Drawing using sketch toolbar of given 3D modeling software.
  • CO2 - Prepare 3D Solid models from 2D sketch using given 3D modeling software.
  • CO3 - Prepare assembly of part models using given 3D modeling software.
  • CO4 - Plot a drawing for given part model/assembly.
  • CO5 - Print components of assembly using 3D Printer/Rapid prototyping machine.
