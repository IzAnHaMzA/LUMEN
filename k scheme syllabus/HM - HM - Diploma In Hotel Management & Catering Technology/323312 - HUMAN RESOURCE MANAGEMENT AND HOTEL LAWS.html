I. RATIONALE

Human resource management and hotel laws are responsible for selecting and inducting competent people, training, facilitating and motivating them to perform at high levels of efficiency at work place. Human resource management would help the students to understand the implications of human resource in hospitality industry, challenges faced and also develop knowledge of the laws governing hotel and hospitality industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to acquire the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: • Adopt and practice employer - employee relationships.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Recognise the role of Human Resource Management in Hotel Industry
  • CO2 - Utilize Job Analysis,Job Description,Specification and sources of recruitment process
  • CO3 - Identify training programs and career planing
  • CO4 - Comprehend the various Hotel Governing Laws
  • CO5 - Apply the licenses and permits required to open Hotel,Restaurant and Bar
  • CO6 - Implement Industrial Acts used in Hotels.
