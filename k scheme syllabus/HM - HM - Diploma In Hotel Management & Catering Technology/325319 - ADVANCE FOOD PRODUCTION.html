I. RATIONALE

This course focuses on the development of advanced skills in International Cuisine. This will help the students acquire skills in kitchen planning, layout, designing kitchen, and recipe balancing. The students will acquire the skill of kitchen stewarding and the Specialized equipment required for International cuisine. These skills will help them to become a specialized chef.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Prepare international cuisines using planning, stewardship, and various equipment.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop kitchen plans for managing effectively.
  • CO2 - Demonstrate the skills required needed in kitchen stewarding
  • CO3 - Use the various equipment used in international cuisine
  • CO4 - Prepare international cuisine
  • CO5 - Incorporate recipe balancing in cuisine.
