I. RATIONALE

Hospitality sector being a service industry, employee has to communicate both with guests and fellow colleagues. In an organization employee’s analyse their strengths and influence their customers timely. While communicating and coordinating, they have to be intelligent emotionally. Accepting and managing challenges in negotiation and conflicts within team or external public. Through this students will inhibit leadership qualities and styles ,and also motivate team members to work in team . And will enable people to come together and share their ideas at workplace and helps the organization to prosper.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: • Implement the concept of Professional skills in hospitality industry
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop life skills for self empowerment
  • CO2 - Exhibit public speaking skills
  • CO3 - Develop professional skills for career building
  • CO4 - Illustrate and perform the leadership styles to motivate team at workplace
  • CO5 - Organize business meetings and conferences with ethics
