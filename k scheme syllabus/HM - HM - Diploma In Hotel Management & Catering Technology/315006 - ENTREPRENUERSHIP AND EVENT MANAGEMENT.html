I. RATIONALE

Entrepreneurs are driving a revolution that is transforming and renewing economies worldwide. This course will help the student to develop entrepreneurship skills, enabling them to establish an enterprise or conceive a start-up. The event business is characteristically a dynamic one, with deliverables and deadlines constantly driving the process of planning. Event management is a popular choice for students planning careers in projects that are diverse and involve many stakeholders. The events are constantly influenced by numerous variables and carry a high level of risk. Risk management is an essential element of event planning and will be developed among the students in this course.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching experiences : IEO Assist Entrepreneurs and Event Management industry in their function.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify entrepreneurship opportunities in hospitality sector
  • CO2 - Plan various activities in event management
  • CO3 - Execute operational activities for effective event management
  • CO4 - Coordinate post-event activities
