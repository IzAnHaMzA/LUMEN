I. RATIONALE

This course focuses on development of advanced skills in continental cuisine. Students will acquire skills in kitchen and production management, layout and designing of stores, frozen desserts and specialized equipment required for continental cuisine, which leads to cultivate the entrepreneurial skills and focuses on sustainability. These skills will help students to become a specialized chef.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Prepare continental cuisines using production management, store control and various equipment.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop menu and prepare continental cuisine
  • CO2 - Utilize variuos specilized equipment used in continental cuisine
  • CO3 - Plan activities in food production management
  • CO4 - Use store control system for kitchen
  • CO5 - Prepare frozen desserts

Micro project
  • Conduct a survey of Continental kitchen's in the city and critique the kitchen's layout with regards to its food style and service and also safety & hygiene point of view individually and submit the report
  • Visit the vegetable market for a survey and list the English (continental) vegetables and fruit's you see and. Classify them in families and record your observations based on seasonal availability and the price range.
  • Conduct survey of hotels for continental kitchen's various categories and restaurants and draw organizational chart of kitchen's staff and also list down the continental dishes served by them,(in a group of 4 student's)
  • Conduct a survey at continental kitchen, observe the continental sauces with their uses in dishes, meat/poultry preparation and prepare the PowerPoint presentation on it (in groups of four)
  • Visit local ice cream factory in your city to study the various types of ice creams available with them (in a group of four )
  • Visit local supermarket store in your city to study the various types of stores control system used by them (in a group of four )
