I. RATIONALE

The course aims to develop specific knowledge & skills required for Baking. It also focuses on creativity in icings & cake decoration. The relevant practical exposure would also be useful to students to begin their career as an entrepreneur by establishing their own Bakery business. The main goal is to provide students with a solid practical knowledge in baking practices, including selection of ingrediants, proper mixing and baking techniques,careful makeup and assembling, skilled and imaginative decoration,and presentation.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences - Establish Baking as a profession.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify Ingredients used in Baking
  • CO2 - Bake various International Breads in Bakery.
  • CO3 - Decorate cakes with different Icings and decoration techniques.
  • CO4 - Prepare Pastry products and their variations.
  • CO5 - Make Cookies and Biscuits by various methods.
