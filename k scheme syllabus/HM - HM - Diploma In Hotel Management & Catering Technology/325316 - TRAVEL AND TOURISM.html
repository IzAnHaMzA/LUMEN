I. RATIONALE

This course in travel and tourism aims to develop knowledge and skills for application in tour planning and management. It helps the students to identify the various impacts of Tourism industry in total. It also enables the students to understand the growth and development of tourism and various factors influencing it by enhancing students' professional knowledge, thus opening doors to more career options in Hotel and Tourism Industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences. •Implement the concept for travel and tourism activities in hospitality Industry
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify motives of travel for different tours.
  • CO2 - Locate infrastructures related to transport, hospitality, and communication.
  • CO3 - Plan itineraries.
  • CO4 - Plan functions of travel agency and identify the basic steps of tour operation.
  • CO5 - Evaluate the impacts of tourism activity on economy, society, culture and environment.
