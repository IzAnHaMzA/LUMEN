I. RATIONALE

Managing finances on hotels starts in the front office. Learning Rooms Division Management is crucial because it's the heart of a hotel, directly impacting revenue, guest satisfaction, and overall operational efficiency. It involves managing front office operations, housekeeping, reservations, and guest services, all contributing to a smooth and enjoyable guest experience. Understanding this area is essential for aspiring hospitality professionals, as it forms the foundation for many career paths within the industry. Through this students will learn how the housekeeping department starts from scratch and is organized for operations in pre-opening property. This course will enable students to get placement as a Guest Service Associate in hotels. Also, students can have their start-up as a facilities management company.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
• The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified outcomes through various teaching learning experiences: Implement the concept of Rooms Division Management in hospitality industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify and prepare various night auditors report
  • CO2 - Evaluate the hotels performance by different reports and statistics
  • CO3 - Utilize the tools and strategies of yield management to maximize revenue generation
  • CO4 - Design the documents for managing housekeeping personnel and execute housekeeping operations
  • CO5 - Identify the various housekeeping expenses and the types of purchasing procedures for various inventory items in housekeeping
  • CO6 - Plan and organize staff, resources and housekeeping procedures for a new property

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Visit the hotel front office and observe the reports and statistics generated at the reservation and reception desk and make the report of the same.
  • Analyse and solve the case studies as a night auditor on night auditing given by the faculty. can use search engines and library books for the same.
  • Use search engines and study the case studies on yield management and give the solutions and submit the report
  • Create an infographic showing the key functions and importance of night audit.
  • Design infographics or charts to represent key front office statistics.
  • Conduct a survey on how hotels use dynamic pricing to optimize revenue.
  • Create the documents - job description, specifications, job lists for Floor Supervisor and GRAs, Housemen in the housekeeping department of five star hotel. Chalk out a orientation and training programme of a week’s duration
  • Use library aids and develop a strategy to reduce costs without affecting service quality.
  • Study the situations/case studies assigned on new property operations and present the report
  • Using search engines design a Material Resource Inventory for a housekeeping department – List essential equipment and supplies needed for operation.
  • Simulate a Pre-Opening Training Program for Housekeeping Staff – Outline a training plan covering cleaning techniques and guest service.
  • Develop an Emergency Cleaning Plan for a Hospital or Museum – Create an action plan for handling spills, damage, or biohazards.
    • Assignment
  • Design the feedback card for the guest (Resident and Non-resident guest of hotel/guest houses/club house) and conduct survey. Submit the report with suggestions and recommendations
  • Create a guide for determining staff strength in housekeeping
  • Prepare a step-by-step budget planning process in housekeeping – Explain the key steps involved in creating a housekeeping budget.
  • Interactive presentation on Yield Management Challenges – Highlight common challenges and propose solutions.
  • Study how room revenue and housekeeping expenses impact a hotel’s financial performance.
  • Visit the hotel and interact with executive housekeeper and acquire knowledge about the budgeting and purchasing operations in department
    • Activities
  • Make a short video explaining the night audit process and its significance.
  • Research how automation has changed the night auditing process over the years.
  • Perform a role play depicting why accurate statistics and reports are essential for decision-making.
  • Invite an Industry expert to deliver a guest lecture on managing housekeeping personnel and planning housekeeping operations
  • Invite Front office manager as an industry expert to the role of yield management
  • Simulation of Job Assignment in a Hotel – Assign roles to a group and observe how tasks are carried out.
  • Prepare a skit on techniques for reducing inventory wastage in Hotels – Explain how hotels minimize losses and maintain optimal stock levels.
  • Simulate an overbooking scenario and analyze how hotels manage it. Conduct a role play.
