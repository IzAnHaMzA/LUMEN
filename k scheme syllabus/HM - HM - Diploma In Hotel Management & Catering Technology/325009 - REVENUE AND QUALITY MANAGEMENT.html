I. RATIONALE

The hospitality industry accommodates tourists where guests stay in a hotel. The basis for charging room rates differs from hotel to hotel. The price band of a particular hotel interests particular segments of patrons. Emphasis is how hotels will arrive at a decision for charging a certain amount as room tariff. For the Revenue manager to understand the Revenue management is critical, as they have to realize how and why consumers use scarce financial resources to make purchasing decisions. Through this, the student will know how a business determines their prices and how their customer perceives prices. Also in hotels meeting guest’s satisfaction and exceeding their expectations are an important part of the hotel business. Total quality management plays an important role in satisfying guests and achieving customer delight. Total Quality requires an immense commitment from employees to analyze guest and employee interaction. Preparation for adopting Total Quality Management is a prerequisite for success. Assessing personnel needs requires identifying the skills and character traits. A good training plan ensures that errors will be reduced because the procedures have been explained and demonstrated to ensure quality services to the guests. Students will develop and enhance their skills through the continuous training process on revenue and quality management.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: • Implement the concept of Revenue and Quality Management in hospitality industry
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Formulate the room tariff of hotel guest rooms
  • CO2 - Implement the practices in Revenue management in hotels
  • CO3 - Incorporate the total quality management approach to the services rendered to guests
  • CO4 - Plan and process the training program to various employees in hotels/front office.
