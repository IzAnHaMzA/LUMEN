I. RATIONALE

Mixology and flair bartending play a crucial role in the bar industry, enhancing both the art of crafting cocktails and entertaining guest. A bar is one of the most important outlets for dispensing alcoholic beverages and serves as a key socializing hub. It is a licensed establishment that offers a wide range of alcoholic drinks to customers, effective bar management encompasses various aspects, including mixology and flair bartending. It targets strategic placement opportunities for skilled professionals, and entrepreneurial ventures in the hospitality industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry identified outcomes: Assist Mixology and Bar Tending industry in their function.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Categorize and select appropriate bar inventory based on operational needs and beverage programs
  • CO2 - Apply advanced mixology techniques and principles to create innovative and high-quality cocktails
  • CO3 - Integrate contemporary flair bartending techniques to enhance service efficiency and customer engagement
  • CO4 - Develop and formulate a range of barista-inspired mixed drinks, demonstrating knowledge of coffee and cocktail synergy
