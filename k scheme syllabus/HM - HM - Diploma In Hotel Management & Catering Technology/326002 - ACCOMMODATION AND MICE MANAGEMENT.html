I. RATIONALE

Today housekeeping departments in hotels witness a realistic change in trends. This led to the industry revising emerging operational dynamics of hotel manpower to derive better value and efficiency. In addition, there is a tremendous use of information technology, new scientific techniques, work studies, and ergonomics. The work profile makes housekeeping an extremely demanding job physically, leading to fatigue. The application and principles of ergonomics make the task of a hotel housekeeper easy and simple. Ecotels also called Green hotels have become a global phenomenon. Through this students will identify how Eco-tel consists of recycled and renovated buildings with upgrades to conserve energy and water and also utilize recovered building materials. Also will understand the process of arranging the physical facilities so that operational efficiency is achieved. And will learn to develop the agreed services that support and improve the effectiveness of the primary activity. Also,the course strives to develop students' professionalism by offering a conceptual overview and methodical study of MICE planning, management, marketing, and applications.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: • Implement the concept of Housekeeping Management in hospitality industry
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify changing trends in housekeeping department and understand applications of Information technology
  • CO2 - Illustrate ergonomics and the benefits of using environment friendly guest supplies and stationery at workplace
  • CO3 - Describe the importance of MICE in hotels
  • CO4 - Identify the latest trends, Services for MICE Guest/Travellers
