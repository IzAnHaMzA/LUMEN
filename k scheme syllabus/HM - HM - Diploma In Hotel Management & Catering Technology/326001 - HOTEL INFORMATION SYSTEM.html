I. RATIONALE

This system helps in managing customer data effectively which organizations in the tourism & Hotel industry can utilize to perform various promotional & direct marketing activities. Information provided by Management information systems helps an organization in management and operational transaction processing, and strategic planning.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help the student to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: Implement and integrate hotel management systems for reservations, guest services, financial reporting, and operations
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use components and functions of Computer system
  • CO2 - Apply the concepts of Microsoft's features
  • CO3 - Identify the components and functions of an MIS and it's multiprocessors environment.
  • CO4 - Analyze the functionality and importance of Hotel management system
  • CO5 - Design the basic structure of HTML for design a web page
