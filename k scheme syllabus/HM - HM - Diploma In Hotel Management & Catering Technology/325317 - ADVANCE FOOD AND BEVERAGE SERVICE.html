I. RATIONALE

Food and Beverage Service is essential in both the accommodation and non-residential catering sectors, with significant growth in the beverage service industry, especially in India. The sector requires a solid understanding of various beverages and service procedures, emphasizing bar operations, destination weddings, and social functions. This course aims to teach students about different spirits, banquets, and buffets.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Prepare and serve alcoholic beverages, as well as to organize catered events and buffets.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Suggest the process of manufacturing whisky and gin
  • CO2 - Select types and brands of rum and brandy
  • CO3 - Select types and brands of vodka and other famous spirits
  • CO4 - Select aperitifs and liqueurs
  • CO5 - Organize banquet functions and buffets
