I. RATIONALE

The hospitality industry is globally the fastest growing industry. To keep up with the ever-changing dynamics,understanding buyer needs, creating marketing strategies and offering competitive pricing will help the learner to develop marketing skills and create a sustainable business model. The skills developed in this course will be helpful for placement and starting of a marketing enterprise.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Segment and target guests using marketing strategies for revenue generation and satisfaction of guest.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select marketing mix for hotels
  • CO2 - Improve customer satisfaction and retention by segmenting, analysing consumer behaviour and improve revenue generation
  • CO3 - Perform marketing research, forecast demand and sale
  • CO4 - Execute product and pricing strategies as per stages in product life cycle
  • CO5 - Frame strategies for effective selection of promotion and distribution channel
