I. RATIONALE

The rising demand for dining out has led to diverse customer needs in the food and beverage industry. With a focus on pairing alcoholic beverages with food, particularly wine, this course equips students with the knowledge and skills needed for effective service and operations. Covering theoretical aspects of wine production, geographically renowned wines, and global wine brands, the course addresses the challenges posed by customers.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry identified outcomes : Assist food and beverage industry in their function.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Categorize alcoholic beverages, along with manufacturing and storage of wines
  • CO2 - Identify French wine producing districts, wine laws and famous brand names of France
  • CO3 - Identify wines of Italy, Germany, Spain and Portugal along with its wine producing districts and famous brands
  • CO4 - Utilize knowledge of new world wine in food and beverage service industry
  • CO5 - Recognize various Spirits and Aperitifs used in food and beverage industry.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Make a folio of white grape varieties with colour pictures and present in classroom.
  • Prepare a project on promoting wines with Indian food.
  • Carry out survey in local area to find out awareness about wines in local people, and present findings in classroom.
  • Prepare a project on wine producing states of India.
  • Make a presentation on wine producing states of India and present in classroom.
  • Prepare vlog on local people awareness about types of wines.
  • Visit nearest winery and prepare a project on it.
    • Expert Talk
  • Invite an expert winemaker as a guest speaker and enlighten the students with their expertise and knowledge, and allow students to submit their learning through report.
  • Invite an expert sommelier as a guest speaker and enlighten the students with their expertise and knowledge of career opportunities in world of wines and allow students to submit their learning through report
    • Assignment
  • Prepare wine list for a five star hotel
  • Prepare world wine map showing wine producing areas
  • Prepare and submit food and wine pairing chart for 13 course French classical menu
  • Search on search engine and present a report on new trends in wine production and service
  • Submit a folio of 30 International wine labels
  • Make and submit a scrapbook of 20 famous aperitifs with colour pictures and its origin
    • Industry Visit
  • Organize an industry visit or a field trip for students to visit the nearest winery/ vineyard and encourage students to compile and submit their learnings in the form of report.
