I. RATIONALE

The confectionery industry offers a promising future for aspiring entrepreneurs, fueled by global trends such as health-conscious indulgence, sustainability, and innovative flavor development. The larder kitchen or Garde Manger is not only dedicated to the preparation of cold foods but also emphasizes the artistic presentations, plating and styling of dishes. This area of culinary arts helps students develop essential professional skills paving the way for strong career opportunities in food production.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Implement professional skills in confectionary and larder work.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Produce a range of confectionery items in chocolate making with sugar artistry
  • CO2 - Prepare and present diverse desserts, frozen desserts, and buffet dessert arrangements
  • CO3 - Execute different operations efficiently within the larder department, including preparation and preservation techniques
  • CO4 - Evaluate the production methods of various charcuterie products, analyzing their quality and characteristics
  • CO5 - Apply the principles of appetizer preparation and garnish application to enhance culinary presentations
