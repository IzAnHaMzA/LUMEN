I. RATIONALE

French is the important communication language in Hospitality Industry. To develop the familiarity with French language is essential for Hotel Management student in order to grasp the basic French terminology. The subject aim to familiarize and to develop basic knowledge in Hospitality French in various departments and also introduces students to the various facets of French.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: • Converse in Hospitality French.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Familiarize with French alphabets, phonetics and vocabulary related to calendar, times and season
  • CO2 - Identify parts of grammar in French
  • CO3 - Develop oral communication skill in French
  • CO4 - Use basic French vocabulary in various departments of Hotel
  • CO5 - Plan menus and recipes in French
