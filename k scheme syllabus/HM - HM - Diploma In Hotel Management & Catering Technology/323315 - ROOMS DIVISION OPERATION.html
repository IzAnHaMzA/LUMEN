I. RATIONALE

Today’s guest expects wow experience during their stay. To satisfy their needs, staff has to be on the toes to create goodwill. Efficient accommodation division and maintenance ensures smooth operations,thereby reducing guest complaints and also enhances staff productivity. Through this students will learn the importance of guest relations and discover how to create first impression, rapport, influence, underline and expandable client needs in terms of ambience desired by the guest.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Implement the concept of rooms division operations in hospitality industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify and handle the guest complaints and problems
  • CO2 - Ensure the safety and security of the guest and handle emergency situations
  • CO3 - Apply the check out procedure with desired mode of bill settlement
  • CO4 - Execute the guest laundry process
  • CO5 - Plan and organize the contract services, renovations and suggest the pest control measures
