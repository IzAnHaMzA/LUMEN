I. RATIONALE

Considering the thriving opportunities and regular demand for Export and Import sector the need for export documentation is increasing with the expanding businesses. It is immensely important to have an insight of International Trade and commerce again making export documentation essential. The course aims to provide information related to the basic concepts of export procedure and documentation. Further, the course informs about customs clearance as well as documentation concerning to international logistics. This export procedure and documentation course focusses on export consignments, export transactions and credit documents. The students will be able to develop skills used in registrations, licenses, procedures and formats regarding export documentation, agencies in documentation. This subject orients the student to carry out those processes and prepare documents for specified tasks in export market.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Identify the export procedures and comply with the export norms during cross border trade.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select relevant registrations to establish textile export oriented firm.
  • CO2 - Prepare the relevant documents required for textile exports.
  • CO3 - Prepare documents to import specified goods.
  • CO4 - Compute the pricing for exports.
  • CO5 - Prepare documents for pre-shipment and post-shipment procedure of specified goods.
