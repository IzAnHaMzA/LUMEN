I. RATIONALE

In the textile industry, fabrics are used as raw materials to manufacture garments and various functional products. The quality of the garments depends on the fabric properties. This course intends to equip students with the concepts, principles and relevant methods and procedures for testing various textile fabrics and garments, which are helpful in selection of raw materials, process control and quality assurance.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Demonstrate the required skills for selecting and assuring the quality of fabric & garment.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Justify the suitability of the fabric based on dimensional and handle properties test results.
  • CO2 - Select relevant fabric based on serviceability properties.
  • CO3 - Test fabric for comfort properties establishing its suitability to garment.
  • CO4 - Apply principles of tensile testing to determine mechanical properties of fabric.
  • CO5 - Apply the principles of garment testing to select relevant accessories and garments.
