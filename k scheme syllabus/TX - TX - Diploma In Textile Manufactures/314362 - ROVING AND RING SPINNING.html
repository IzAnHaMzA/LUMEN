I. RATIONALE

A comprehensive understanding of roving and ring spinning is vital for textile technologists. Roving spinning converts fibers into roving, which is then processed in ring spinning to produce yarn, influencing properties like strength, imperfections and evenness. Expertise of these processes enables improvements in yarn production and enhances competitiveness in the market.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Implement the process of fibre drafting, twisting and building of rove and yarn to produce quality roving and yarn in the spinning industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use Speed frame machine to produce roving from the draw frame sliver.
  • CO2 - Apply mechanism of roving build to achieve proper winding of rove on the roving bobbin.
  • CO3 - Use Ring frame machine to produce yarn from the roving.
  • CO4 - Apply mechanism of cop build to achieve proper winding of yarn on the ring bobbin.
  • CO5 - Use monitoring systems of ring frame to monitor and control the production as well as the quality of yarn.
