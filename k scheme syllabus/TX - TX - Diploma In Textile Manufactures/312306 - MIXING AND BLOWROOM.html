I. RATIONALE

In spinning, the knowledge of cotton ginning and baling process, opening, cleaning and mixing of fibres is of prime importance to manufacture yarn from fibres. The opening, cleaning and mixing of fibres affect on properties of yarn produced in spinning, and furthermore, it affects fabric properties. So, it is essential for textile technologists to learn the principles of machines involved in blow room. This course describes basic facts, concepts, and principles of opening and cleaning of fibres.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Optimize the principle of opening and cleaning of fibres to produce superior quality of yarn.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use cotton cultivation and harvesting process to produce raw material for spinning
  • CO2 - Use ginning and baling machines to convert raw cotton to bale
  • CO3 - Apply the principles of opening and cleaning for raw material.
  • CO4 - Use different blow room machines for optimum opening, cleaning and mixing of fibres
  • CO5 - Use different methods of lap preparation to produce lap
