I. RATIONALE

A comprehensive understanding of the Carding, Draw Frame, Comber preparatory processes, and Comber is imperative for textile technologists aiming to produce desired yarn characteristics. These processes are pivotal for fiber individualization, parallelization, and the elimination of shorter fibers, which directly impact yarn quality. This course provides essential knowledge and skills on these principles for optimizing yarn quality and production.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Optimize the principle of fiber individualization, parallelization, and the elimination of shorter fibers to produce quality yarn in the spinning industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use carding machine to individualize the fibres, eliminate trash and dust from fibre material.
  • CO2 - Use Auto-leveler at card to produce uniform sliver.
  • CO3 - Apply principle of drafting to parallelize the fibres in sliver.
  • CO4 - Use comber preparatory machines to produce even lap.
  • CO5 - Use comber to produce even sliver by removing short fibres.
