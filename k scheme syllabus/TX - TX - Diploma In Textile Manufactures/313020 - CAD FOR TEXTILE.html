I. RATIONALE

Artistic design demands skill, utilizing principles like composition and color theory. Creative ideas capture market value when expressed with direction. In contrast, textile design must blend technical viability with aesthetic beauty, influencing fabric quality and cost. Computer Adaid Design(CAD) system streamlines textile design, enhancing efficiency for students, designers, and manufacturers, delivering superior fashion products to the market. The software serves as a natural extension of the designer, fostering practical skills for innovative textile designs.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Employ artistic skills, design principles, and CAD efficiently to create market-worthy, technically sound, and aesthetically pleasing textile designs.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply various principles of design to produce textile articles
  • CO2 - Apply theories of colour, modified colour, colour harmony and various compositions of design for garment applications
  • CO3 - Create a point paper design for Dobby and Jacquard
  • CO4 - Use relevant modules of CAD software to generate data sheets and fabric simulations.
