I. RATIONALE

In textile industry, the fibre is used as raw material to manufacture yarn and nonwoven fabric. Quality of yarn depends on the fibre properties. This course intends to equip students with the concepts, principles and relevant methods for testing various textile fibres, which are helpful for selection of raw materials, process control and quality assurance. Fibre testing requires recording number of observations, which are to be analyzed, interpreted and used for best results.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Employ relevant fibre testing principles, methods for selection of raw material, process control and quality assurance.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select relevant sampling method for fibre testing.
  • CO2 - Evaluate fibre based on moisture relation.
  • CO3 - Select fibre based on fibre length and fineness.
  • CO4 - Select fibre based on fibre maturity and strength.
  • CO5 - Analyze neps, trash content and grade the cotton.
