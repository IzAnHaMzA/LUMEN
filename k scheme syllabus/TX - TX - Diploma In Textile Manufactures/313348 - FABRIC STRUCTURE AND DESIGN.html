I. RATIONALE

Diploma engineers have to deal with different weaving machines to reproduce various types of fabric design on loom.The study of basic concepts and principles of fabric structure will help them to reproduce good quality fabric.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Develop different fabric structure designs for textile industry by using principles of Interlacment.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop different drafts and peg plans using basics of fabric structure.
  • CO2 - Develop relevant derivatives of plain weave.
  • CO3 - Construct relevant derivatives of twill weave.
  • CO4 - Create different designs by using principle of sateen weave.
  • CO5 - Develop innovative fabric designs by using fancy structure.
