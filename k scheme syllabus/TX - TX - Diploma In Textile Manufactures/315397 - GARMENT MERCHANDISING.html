I. RATIONALE

The importance of merchandising is growing in the garment industry, both domestically and internationally. Merchandisers act as key links between buyers and manufacturers, handling tasks like order procurement, order finalization and product merchandising. Students has to inculcate the roles and responsibilities of merchandiser, which further helps them to make merchandising plans for both domestic and export markets.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Function as a merchandiser in the apparel industry for smooth execution of the orders.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Manage merchandising activities with different department.
  • CO2 - Perform key responsibilities of different types of merchandisers.
  • CO3 - Evaluate product development of apparel industry.
  • CO4 - Prepare the plan for merchandising process.
  • CO5 - Develop sourcing and costing strategy for apparel industry.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Establish contact with a relevant alumnus and gather merchandising materials, such as tech packs, Bills of Material (BOMs), and other relevant documents. Conduct a thorough analysis of the collected materials to comprehend the terminology and content. Compile your findings into a comprehensive report and submit it for evaluation.
  • Prepare a report and submit roles and responsibilities of merchandiser-Select a college alumnus working in merchandising and prepare a questionnaire to gather insights into their roles and responsibilities. Collect valuable data and information that will aid in future interviews. Additionally, request soft copies of relevant documents, including - Tech packs - Time and action calendars - Bills of Material (BOMs) - Other merchandising materials to gain a comprehensive understanding of merchandising processes and best practices.
  • Prepare a report on retail Merchandising- Visit two branded retail showrooms and observe their clothing displays. Inquire about their retail merchandising processes and procedures. Based on your observations and findings, create a detailed report documenting the merchandising strategies employed by each showroom.
  • Conduct an on-site visit to a nearby garment industry and compile a comprehensive report detailing the functions, processes, and activities of various departments, providing insight into the industry's operational dynamics.
    • Assignment
  • Prepare a power point presentation and present on different types of samples of product development department.

  • Prepare a detailed cost sheet of any one branded product (shirt /Trouser/ T-shirt).
  • Prepare specification sheet of any one branded product (shirt /Trouser/ T-shirt).
