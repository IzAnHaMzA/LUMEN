I. RATIONALE

Mechanical, electrical and electronics appliances, equipment are the most essential inputs of any textile industry. The diploma engineer must have knowledge of machines, materials, mechanisms, transmission systems, different types of motors and their working, billing of electrical energy and the safety measures while working in textile industry. Along with this, textile/garment machine manufacturers have introduced many electronic devices for various purposes to indicate, to measure and to control various units of textile/garment processes. This course is developed in the way by which fundamental information will help the diploma engineers to apply the basic concepts and principles of mechanical, electrical and electronic engineering in various engineering applications to solve broad based problems in textile industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Solve broadly based problems in the textile industry by using the basic principles of Mechanical, Electrical and Electronics engineering.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply the laws of machine and material in textile/garment machines.
  • CO2 - Select relevant transmission drives for textile/garment machines.
  • CO3 - Estimate the energy consumption to solve energy bill problems.
  • CO4 - Identify relevant transformers and motors used in textile industry.
  • CO5 - Identify different types of electronic components and semiconductor devices used in textile industry .
  • CO6 - Identify sensors and actuators for relevant textile process.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Collect technical specifications for different solar panels used in textile industry and prepare a chart/presentation.
  • Each batch will visit various industry/department and estimate their energy bills.
  • Each batch will prepare detailed specifications of temperature sensors, pressure sensors, optical sensors, displacement sensors used in textile/garment industry
  • Prepare presentation incorporating animation displaying different transducers used in textile industry.
  • Prepare presentation incorporating detailed information of actuators, relays, contactors, solenoids used in different processing units.
  • Each batch will prepare model of gear train useful for textile/garment machines.
    • Assignment
  • Each batch will collect and prepare chart to required specifications and rating of motors used in various textile/garment industry
  • Each batch will visit, collect and prepare chart to required specifications of transformers used in textile/garment industry
  • Each batch will prepare resistor colour code charts. Use the chart to calculate values of different resistors. Collect information of variable resistors, rheostats used in laboratory.
  • Each batch will prepare list of active/passive components, semiconductor devices used in different textile units spinning, weaving, sizing, dying, and testing. List their applications and specifications.
  • Prepare chart indicating mechanical properties of different materials used in textile/garment plants.
