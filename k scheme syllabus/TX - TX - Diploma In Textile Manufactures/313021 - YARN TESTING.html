I. RATIONALE

In textile industry, the yarn is used as raw material to manufacture woven, knitted and braided fabric. Quality of fabric depends on the yarn properties. This course intends to equip students with the concepts, principles and relevant methods for testing various textile yarns, which are helpful for selection of raw materials, process control and quality assurance.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Employ the yarn testing principles and methods for process control, and quality assurance in the textile industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply yarn numbering system to determine its linear density.
  • CO2 - Determine twist parameters by diffrent testing methods.
  • CO3 - Measure yarn hairiness and unevenness by relevant method.
  • CO4 - Apply principles of tensile testing to determine yarn strength.
  • CO5 - Evaluate physical properties of syntheic yarns.
