I. RATIONALE

This course equips the diploma engineers with essential knowledge and skills in shuttleless weaving technologies, including projectile, rapier, airjet, and water jet methods. Using these principles, diploma engineers become capable to operate modern weaving machines effectively and efficiently, ensuring high-quality fabric production in large quantities.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use Shuttleless weaving machines technology to produce relevant fabric in the weaving industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select relevant shuttleless weaving machines for given fabric requirements.
  • CO2 - Utilize the Projectile weaving machine technology to manufacture the fabric with relevant process parameters.
  • CO3 - Utilize the Rapier weaving machine technology to manufacture the fabric with relevant process parameters.
  • CO4 - Utilize the Jet weaving machine technology to manufacture the fabric with relevant process parameters.
  • CO5 - Use novel methods of weaving for producing special quality fabrics selecting appropriate raw materials and preparatory processes.
