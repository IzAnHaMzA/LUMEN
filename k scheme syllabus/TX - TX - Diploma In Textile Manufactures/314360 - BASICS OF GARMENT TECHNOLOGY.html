I. RATIONALE

The Garment manufacturing process requires numerous machineries for their manufacture. Also certain super-specialized machineries are used in this field. This course introduces these machineries and uses, their assemblies & parts in detail. Student will undergo the different process sequence steps in garment industry and comprehend different sewing machine settings that will help the student to overcome the problems in production of garments.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply the principles of garment manufacturing to manufacture different garments.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Prepare basic bodice blocks and markers according to body measurements.
  • CO2 - Cut layers of fabrics according to marker after making the layer by spreading the fabric.
  • CO3 - Stitch different garment parts to create three dimensional garments.
  • CO4 - Identify different trims and accessories used for garment manufacturing and garment costing.
  • CO5 - Apply different finishing processes for garments to obtain desired qualities.
