I. RATIONALE

Textile mathematics & data analysis plays a crucial role in practicing of units, conversions to other measurement systems, graphical interpretation & control charts. By utilizing components of Textile mathematics & data analysis, students will be able systemize textile manufacturing process analytically. This course helps students develop their decision-making skills regarding production problems.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply the concept of Textile mathematics & data analysis for day-to-day industry practices.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use suitable units of textile material to estimate output of various machines.
  • CO2 - Solve industry-based technological problems using the basic concept of Mathematical Statistics.
  • CO3 - Develop tabular forms ,graphs & statistical control charts for given numerical data to solve textile related problems.
  • CO4 - Use correlation & linear regression to solve textile related problems.
  • CO5 - Apply the law of motion & machines to solve industry-based technology problems.
