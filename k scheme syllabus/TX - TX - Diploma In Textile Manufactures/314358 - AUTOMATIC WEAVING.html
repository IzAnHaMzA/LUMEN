I. RATIONALE

It is essential to understand the working of components in automatic looms to produce high-quality woven fabric. The course focuses on applying the principles of weaving machines to produce fabrics and solve industry-related problems with looms. This course also helps the students to produce various weaves in the woven fabric by utilizing principles of dobbies and jacquard mechanism.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Produce woven fabric using automatic looms with relevant shedding mechanisms in the weaving industry
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop the fancy woven structure using conventional dobbies.
  • CO2 - Apply modern dobby principles to create given fabric designs.
  • CO3 - Use a conventional jacquard mechanism to produce complex woven fabric designs.
  • CO4 - Apply the principles of modern jacquard and drop box mechanism to produce colored figures in woven fabric.
  • CO5 - Develop a quality fabric using an automatic weaving machine.
