I. RATIONALE

Textile wet processing plays a crucial role in elevating the aesthetic, performance and market value of fabrics which depends on pre-treatments, dyeing, printing, and finishing processes. This course aims to enhance student's understanding ability of textile wet processing which makes use of various chemicals coupled with providing basic knowledge about pre-treatment, dyeing, printing and finishing processes of textiles.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Solve broad based textile manufacturing problems by adopting chemical wet processing.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Handle pretreatment of fabrics for perfect desizing, scouring and bleaching of textile goods based on the substrate type.
  • CO2 - Select relevant dyes and dyeing methods for dyeing of given commercial cellulosic textiles.
  • CO3 - Use relevant dyes and dyeing methods for dyeing of commercial protein and synthetic textiles.
  • CO4 - Prepare print paste for printing of commercial cellulosic and synthetic textiles using relevant ingredients.
  • CO5 - Use mechanical and chemical finishing process for commercial fabrics processing.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Collect any two cotton dyed samples from industry with their recipe and produce same results in laboratory on OBBD machine by changing appropriate recipe. Correlate the results and present.
  • Visit industries and collect dyed samples of faulty dyeing and find remedies to rectify same. Present your report.
  • Video preparation: - Visit the local industry and prepare a video of any one machine used for the preparation of fabric.
  • Compare the effect on dyeing of bleached and unbleached samples dyed with reactive dye.
  • Collection of pre-treated samples: Collect pre-treated samples at various stages. Write a report on changes that take place at every stage.
  • Visit textile printing industries/ market shops and collect at least 15 different printed samples of cotton/ silk/ wool fabrics. Classify them with respect to method of printing and class of dye used for printing. Present the results.
  • Visit different textile printing industries. Observe the working operations of machine at various stages and capture those processes using video camera. Edit the videos as per specified process sequence and make a short film. Present the short film.
  • Library/ internet survey regarding fabrics treated with various finishes used in different industries.
  • Prepare presentation incorporating visuals, photographs, animations, video on process sequence of finishing natural fibres.
  • Prepare a report of dyeability of bleached and unbleached cotton fabric using reactive dyes and present the same.
    • Assignment
  • Arrange the acids and alkalis used in various textile pre-treatments. Write their role in a particular process.
  • List the chemicals and auxiliaries used in textile printing. Write the role of each ingredient in a particular process.
  • Prepare table for different chemicals used in fabric finishing of cotton and various natural fabrics and their effects.
  • Organize the chemicals used in dyeing of various class of dyes on fabric types. Write the role of chemicals used during the process.
