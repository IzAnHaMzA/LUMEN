I. RATIONALE

Diploma textile engineers have to deal with different yarn winding machines and techniques to manufacture various types of yarns. The study of basic concepts of yarn count, principles of winding , different supply & delivery packages and different yarn clearers will help them to produce good winding packages efficiently and economically to the industrial society. The efficient and economical manufacturing of various winding packages will be beneficial to industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
"Use the basic principles of yarn winding to prepare the relevant packages for warping, shuttless weaving, traditional weaving and knitting.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop relevent Manufacturing process sequence for relevent woven fabrics.
  • CO2 - Estimate the linear density of yarn by using the principles of yarn numbering system.
  • CO3 - Use the principles of winding to convert small package to large package.
  • CO4 - Select relevent knotting/splicing technicque to convert small packages to large packages.
  • CO5 - Estimate yarn winding parameters to produce the different packages.
