I. RATIONALE

Packaging is an essential medium for preserving food quality, minimizing food wastage and reducing preservatives used in food. The food technologist is required to have the knowledge of packaging material, its function, properties, types, forms, suitability to foods, quality of packaging material, novel packaging systems, migration quality aspects and legislative aspects. This knowledge shall be used for select suitable packaging material and packaging system and apply the food packaging labeling as per regulation

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Select and use suitable packaging material and method of packaging as per the type of food product and design label as per FSSAI regulation.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Understand the functions, advantages and applications of food packaging materials.
  • CO2 - Describe the food packaging materials and its manufacturing.
  • CO3 - Evaluate the quality of food packaging material.
  • CO4 - Use of recent packaging systems and technology in food processing.
  • CO5 - Design the food packaging labels and apply FSSAI labelling regulations.
