I. RATIONALE

Spices are in high demand globally for culinary, medicinal, and cosmetic uses. Processing adds value to raw spices, enhancing profitability for producers and exporters. This course covers the processing of major and minor spices processing, its value-added product, standards, spices processing, and packaging equipment’s. This will help to encourage entrepreneurship by providing knowledge on spices processing, and setting up and managing spice processing units.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences:- • Prepare the various spices and its value-added products as per FSSAI standards.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the sources of spices and certification requirement spices processing Industry.
  • CO2 - Demonstrate the preparation of major spices processing and its value-added products.
  • CO3 - Demonstrate the preparation of minor spices processing and its value-added products.
  • CO4 - Describe the construction and working of spices processing and packaging equipment’s.
  • CO5 - Prepare the various spices processing products as per FSSAI standard.
