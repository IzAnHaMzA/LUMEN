I. RATIONALE

Supply chain management is crucial for any industry, but it's especially vital in the food sector due to the perishable nature of its products. This course focuses on key areas such as sourcing, procurement, demand forecasting, inventory management, transportation modes, food logistics, and designing distribution networks. By learning these fundamentals, students gain practical skills to efficiently manage supply chains in the food industry while ensuring profitability and safety.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to be help the student to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experience: Manage food supply chains, ensuring profitability and safety.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Outline components of supply chain in food industry.
  • CO2 - Design appropriate distribution network for food industry.
  • CO3 - Use appropriate sourcing and procurement models.
  • CO4 - Apply suitable technologies for food logistics.
  • CO5 - Use demand forecasting and inventory management as tools to manage supply chain.
