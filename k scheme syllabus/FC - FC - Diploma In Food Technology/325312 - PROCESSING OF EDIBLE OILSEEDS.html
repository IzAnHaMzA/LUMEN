I. RATIONALE

This subject deals with the details of the production process of edible oil and its value-added products which are generally employed in the food Industry. This course covers oilseed Chemistry, Post-processing, Mechanical oil extraction, Solvent oil extraction, edible oil refining, FSSAI guidelines for oil processing and packaging, and value-added products from oil. This will help to encourage entrepreneurship by providing knowledge on spices processing, and setting up and managing spice processing units.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The course aims to help students achieve industry-identified competencies in oil extraction from edible seeds per FSSAI standards.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Prepare raw materials for oil extraction using post-processing steps.
  • CO2 - Apply solvent extraction for edible oil..
  • CO3 - Apply chemical and physical treatments to refine edible oil.
  • CO4 - Preserve edible oil quality according to FSSAI guidelines.
  • CO5 - Prepare value-added products from edible oil.
