I. RATIONALE

This course imparts the knowledge of utilization, milling , baking, and confectionery technology. It deals with different processes and methods used to prepare bakery and confectionery products. This knowledge shall be used for select the proper ingredients and prepare various bakery, snack, breakfast cereals, and confectionery products

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to be help the student to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experience: Manufacture of bakery, confectionery, snack, and breakfast cereals products as per FSSAI standards.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - State the importance, nutritional composition of cereal grains and millets.
  • CO2 - Outline the milling process of cereal grains.
  • CO3 - Select proper ingredients to prepare different bakery products.
  • CO4 - Describe production process pasta product and snack food processing.
  • CO5 - Utilize sugar and other ingredients to prepare hard boiled candy and chocolate.
