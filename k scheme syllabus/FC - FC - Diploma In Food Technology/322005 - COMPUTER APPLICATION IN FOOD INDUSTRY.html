I. RATIONALE

This course is essential in the food processing industry for process control, data analysis, and managing business operations. This subject covers basic information about software used in the food industry, reliable sources for literature reviews, information sources in food technology, solving food processing problems, and statistical analysis using spreadsheets. This course requires prior knowledge of basic computer fundamentals

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use computer for data analysis and problem solving in food processing.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use relevant application software’s used in food industry.
  • CO2 - Perform a literature survey in the field of food technology through authentic websites.
  • CO3 - Prepare formats and chart with calculation by use of formulas in spreadsheet.
  • CO4 - Solve the data base problems in food processing by using spreadsheet.
