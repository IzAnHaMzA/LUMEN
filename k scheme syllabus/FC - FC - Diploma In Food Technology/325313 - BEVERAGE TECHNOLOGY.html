I. RATIONALE

This course deals with the technologies used in manufacturing different types of beverages. It majorly includes packaged drinking water, mineral water, fruits and vegetable-based beverages, carbonated beverages and alcoholic beverages. It also talks about the recent trends in the beverage industry and FSSAI regulations governing it. This course will help students to understanding the technology behind the beverages while ensuring their quality and safety.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: • Prepare, preserve, and package different types of food beverages.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Design manufacturing process of packaged drinking water.
  • CO2 - Prepare fruits and vegetables based beverages as per FSSAI standards.
  • CO3 - Prepare carbonated beverages as per FSSAI standards.
  • CO4 - Prepare alcoholic beverages as per FSSAI standards.
  • CO5 - Outline formulations for new beverages product.
