I. RATIONALE

Human Nutrition is a core subject in Food Technology, which outlines one of the basic needs of the human body. It introduces students to the types of nutrients, their roles, functions, sources, utilization in the human body, and ill effects on health if balance and moderation are not maintained. The latest RDA tables, the healthy eating plate, and reading food labels for Nutrition facts have been included so that students can make wise decisions and prepared Nutrient rich food products

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Formulate a balanced diet by identifying sources, functions, RDA values, and calorific value of nutrients for controlling deficiency disorders
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Outlines terms related to human nutrition.
  • CO2 - Identify the sources, functions, and digestion, absorption, and deficiency disorders of macronutrients.
  • CO3 - Identify the sources, classification, functions and deficiency disorders of micronutrients
  • CO4 - Calculate calorific value of food and energy needs of body as per RDA.
  • CO5 - Formulate a balanced diet for different stages of normal life cycle.
