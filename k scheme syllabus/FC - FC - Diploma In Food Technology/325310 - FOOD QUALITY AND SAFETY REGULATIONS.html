I. RATIONALE

This course deals with the systems and techniques used to maintain the quality and safety of food. It primarily includes the quality attributes of food, techniques for sensory evaluation, and food safety systems like HACCP, which ensure food safety. The course also addresses food safety regulations in India that are mandatory for any food business operator. It will help students in maintaining the quality and safety of food products.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences:- • Evaluate quality and ensure safety of food.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Measure quality attributes of food.
  • CO2 - Evaluate quality of food using sensory evaluation
  • CO3 - Prepare HACCP plan for ensuring safety of food
  • CO4 - Evaluate compliance of food with respect to licensing, prohibitions and food recall regulation under FSSAI
  • CO5 - Evaluate compliance of food with respect to labeling, health supplements, organic and fortified foods under FSSAI
