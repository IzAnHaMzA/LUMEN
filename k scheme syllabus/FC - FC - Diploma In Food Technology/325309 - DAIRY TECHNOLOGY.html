I. RATIONALE

This course deals with the milk processing, preparation of special milks, preparation of milk products and its quality control as per FSSAI standards. It also covers quality analysis of raw milk, chemistry and microbiology of milk, milk standardization, homogenization, pasteurization process and preparation of major dairy products. This course helps student to start own enterprise in ice-cream, frozen dessert, special milks processing and Indian dairy products.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: • Manufacture various milk and milk products as per FSSAI standards.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Describe the physic-chemical properties of milk constituents and milk.
  • CO2 - Testing the raw milk quality and use pasteurization process for market milk.
  • CO3 - Outline the production process of major special milks.
  • CO4 - Prepare the Ice –Cream, Cheese, and Condensed Milk as per FSSAI standards.
  • CO5 - Prepare the various Indian traditional dairy products as per FSSAI standards.
