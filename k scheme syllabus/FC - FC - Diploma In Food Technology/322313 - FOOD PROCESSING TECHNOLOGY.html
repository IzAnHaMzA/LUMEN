I. RATIONALE

This subject deals with the major unit operations which are generally used for raw materials preparation, to final product preparation. This course covers Cleaning, Sorting, Grading, Blanching, Size reduction, Emulsification, Homogenization, Mixing, Extrusion, Filtration, Centrifugation, Evaporation, membrane separation, and Material handling equipment in food processing. This will help to select and apply an appropriate unit operation to prepare the particular food product.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
• Use various unit operations and processes in the production of various food products.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Prepare good-quality raw materials by using pre-processing equipment’s.
  • CO2 - Select and use the appropriate unit operations of size reduction and homogenization for food processing.
  • CO3 - Apply the Mixing and extrusion operation during food processing.
  • CO4 - Prepare the food product by using a separation and concentration process.
  • CO5 - Outline the material handling equipment's in the food industry.
