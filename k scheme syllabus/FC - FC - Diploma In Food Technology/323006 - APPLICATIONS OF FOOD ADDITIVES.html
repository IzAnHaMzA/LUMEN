I. RATIONALE

This course is essential in food processing industry for improving the quality and safety of food by using the suitable food additive having specific technological function. This subject covers the application of different food additives such as stabilizers, thickeners, colorants, preservatives, antioxidants, sweeteners, acidity regulators, emulsifiers etc. This course will equip students with skills required for standardizing a food formulation for desired quality and safety parameters.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use suitable food additives for intended technological benefit
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the food categorization code of any food product as per FSSAI & Codex database.
  • CO2 - Outline functions of given functional classes of food additives
  • CO3 - Select food additive for particular technological function.
  • CO4 - Identify maximum level of use of given food additive in given type of food.
