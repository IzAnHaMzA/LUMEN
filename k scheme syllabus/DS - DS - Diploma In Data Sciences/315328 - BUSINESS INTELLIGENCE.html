I. RATIONALE

This course focuses on understanding the basics of business intelligence to collect, integrate, analyze, and apply BI concepts.It also provides students with theoretical knowledge and practical skills for managing data modeling concepts and enterprise reporting advantages for better and more informed business decisions.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use any Business Intelligence tool for managing data modeling concepts and enterprise reporting.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Describe Business Intelligence and its framework.
  • CO2 - Apply Business Intelligence Planning Process.
  • CO3 - Analyze Business Intelligence Database Design.
  • CO4 - Implement Extraction ,Transformation and Loading process.
  • CO5 - Develop Business application and generate its Report.
