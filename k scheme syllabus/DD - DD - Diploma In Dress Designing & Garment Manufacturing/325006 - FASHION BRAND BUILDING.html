I. RATIONALE

Brand development is strategic process of creating and distributing the company's image products and services. It includes aligning the brand with the business objectives, communicating the brand to the largest market and updating the brand as necessary. The student will gain the insights related to the brands, customers, market, competition and implement effective brand strategies, drive business growth, and excel in their marketing and branding careers.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to achieve the following industry identified outcome through various learning experiences: Develop comprehensive brand strategies to create compelling fashion brand identities based on market research, consumer insights, repositioning strategies, brand reputation, crisis, and social responsibility.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply the concepts of brand & branding for fashion products .
  • CO2 - Identify the various brand identities and key components of successful brand strategies.
  • CO3 - Develop brand identities based on brand positions, personalities and brand positioning statements.
  • CO4 - Measure the brand equity and value using various models and metrics.
  • CO5 - Analyze brand life cycle stages suggesting steps for growth and revitalization.
  • CO6 - Apply ethical principles and spiritual values in branding.
