I. RATIONALE

This course applies the art movements fashion ideas in a visual form, that enables to create application ideas through different figure forms that, including quick stylized poses, texture, print, draping, accessories, garment with suitable color interpretation.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning: "Apply of art appreciation movements to various fashion stylized poses using the appropriate drawing elements.”
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop proportionate fashion sketches using quick sketching techniques and weight distribution.
  • CO2 - Apply Gesalt Law to design prints and textures.
  • CO3 - Analyze three dimensional fabric silhouette through live drape.
  • CO4 - Apply art movements through motifs, prints and texture on garments and accessories.
  • CO5 - Illustrate the garment inspired from Indian traditional painting.
