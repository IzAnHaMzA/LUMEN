I. RATIONALE

The course aims to provide concepts related to use photoshop to create and optimize graphics specifically for fashion designing industry. Adobe Photoshop is software that is extensively used for raster image editing, graphic design and digital art. It makes use of layering to allow for depth and flexibility in the design and editing process, as well as provide powerful editing tools. It will help students to compose, modify & filter mood boards, story boards in a creative manner.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to expand the digital designing in clothing industry & market, apply Photoshop tools and techniques for industrial application.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select various tools and techniques of Photoshop. (scan, edit, save, & printing.)
  • CO2 - Apply layers & masking of layer for industrial documents (Business card, Digital illustration Fashion catalogue)
  • CO3 - Apply collage techniques, text effects, Create Mockup templates to design advertisement, Fashion catalogue, label design, packaging etc
  • CO4 - Create Mood board, research board by composing, modifying, applying various filter effects, distortion, Dispersion effects
  • CO5 - Apply Mash up, 3D effects to develop Posters, web design, social media post /advertisement, & label design for any clothing brand
