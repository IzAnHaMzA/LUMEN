I. RATIONALE

This course applies the knowledge of wardrobe designing to implementing the basic concepts of designing and styling to create seasonal design collections, client profiling and standardized concept boards for the fashion industries.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: • Apply principles of design to create customized design of garments.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop various fashion boards with technical aspects.
  • CO2 - Develop various client profiles based on identified parameters.
  • CO3 - Select texture and color scheme suitable to various seasonal wear.
  • CO4 - Design wardrobe by using various media and technique.
