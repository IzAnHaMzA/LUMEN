I. RATIONALE

Indian textile and embroidery plays a vital role in the fashion industry. This incorporates the rich history and substantial cultural value of fashion. This course develops the skill of Indian traditional embroidery giving emphasis on garment embellishment with comprehensive guidelines to identify colors, motifs and textures as per the traditional Indian practices. Indian textile and embroidery enhances the traditional and contemporary designing abilities of the student.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning:Develop traditional and contemporary patterns through the analysis of Indian embroidery and textile.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select appropriate tools, material, motifs, color combination and stitch type for hand embroidery.
  • CO2 - Apply the fundamentals of embroidery to create embroidery motifs.
  • CO3 - Identify the origin, fabric, thread, colors, Stitches, Motifs of Regional Indian embroidery (Kantha of Bengal, Kasuti of Karnataka and Phulkari of Punjab)
  • CO4 - Identify the origin, fabric, thread, colors, Stitches, Motifs of Regional Indian embroidery (Kashida of Kashmir, Chikankari of Uttar Pradesh and Kutch and Kathiawar of Gujarat)
  • CO5 - Design contemporary fashion garments using inspiration from traditional Western and Eastern Indian textiles patterns..
  • CO6 - Design contemporary fashion garments using inspiration from traditional Southern and Northern Indian textiles patterns.
