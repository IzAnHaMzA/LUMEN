I. RATIONALE

Innovation is the process that brings together novel ideas and solutions that meet new requirements or already existing needs. Innovation is vital for the sustainable development of all sectors of human society. This course will help students to develop fabrics using innovative materials and techniques that can be exclusively used for creative designs. This will enhance the look of the fabric to give impactful visibility and enable students to survive in a dynamic fashion environment with a constant focus on innovation.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: • Develop fabrics using innovative materials and surface ornamentation techniques.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select various Print Designs for dyeing and Printing purposes.
  • CO2 - Apply prints on fabrics using various Printing methods.
  • CO3 - Create Printed fabrics using Tie & Dye and Batic techniques.
  • CO4 - Identify Innovative Technical Textiles.
  • CO5 - Use Eco-friendly Innovative dyeing and Printing techniques.
  • CO6 - Develop eco-friendly innovative Prints.
