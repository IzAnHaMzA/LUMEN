I. RATIONALE

Advanced pattern drafting is gaining importance in the fashion industry. Pattern drafting is the art of applying design, aesthetics, clothing construction and natural beauty to clothing and its accessories. It is influenced by culture and different trends, and has varied over time and place. The course provides training in garment production aligned with the fashion industry standards, also equipping the students with skills in predicting popular styles and selecting appropriate fabrics for men's tailoring. Additionally, this provides students the skills to ensure proper fitting for men's clothing in different environments and occasion. The curriculum includes topics such as designing, predicting trends, pattern making, and fabric knowledge.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences: Implement advance garment-construction techniques in the Men's garment manufacturing industries.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop Upper Body Garments using principles and techniques of pattern making .
  • CO2 - Develop Lower Body Garments using principles and techniques of pattern making .
  • CO3 - Estimate the consumption of fabrics using different types of lay-marking techniques in various Men.
  • CO4 - Construct innovative garments for the Men's wear using industrial sewing & cutting machine.
  • CO5 - Develope Men’s wear garments in compliance with Industrial specifications.

Self Learning
  • Prepare report on quality control operation in a men's wear garment production industry .
  • Prepare Flow-charts and technical drawing of any two men's wear garment's.
    • Micro project
  • Collect photos to develop a library of the different types of seam finishes, sewing techniques, and hems in construction of Men's wear.
  • Using fabrics as a source of inspiration to create any five designs for a group or collection of Men's wear for specific occasion.
  • Prepare a PPT presentation stating Sustainable fabrics in an eco-friendly environment and ethical production of Men's wear.
  • Compile a report of various Facings and Button Extensions, Buttonholes, Buttons and fasteners used in production of Men's wear.
  • Conduct a market survey or e-visit and compile a report on current trends and brand Preference Towards Men's Wear.
  • Identify and rectify men's lower garments fitting problems (any five fitting problems).
  • Identify and rectify men's upper garments fitting problems (any five fitting problems)
    • Assignment
  • Develop visual databank of garment pressing purposes and categories of pressing.
  • Collect photos or develop a library of the different types of seam finishes, sewing techniques, and hems in construction of Men's wear.
  • Repurposing Assignment: Students should repurpose an old garment into a new one (simple) to encourage creativity and sustainability.
  • Finishing Techniques Identification Assignment: Students should visit the brand mall, outlets, stores and boutiques to analyze brands finishing techniques used in Men's wear.
  • Visit a men's wear garment production industry .
  • Prepare a report on in the given prescribed format (types of garments, designing and styling elements considered, and product price categories)of any designer label.
