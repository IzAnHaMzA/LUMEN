I. RATIONALE

This course aims at creating awareness amongst the students about global level commitment towards sustainable development and implementing ethics in the production process with respect to supply chain, the environmental and social impacts, carbon footprints , safety and fair deal towards the workforce involved at all levels.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use ethical sourcing and sustainable practices in supply chain.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the challenges and probable solutions in ethical and sustainable garment sourcing.
  • CO2 - Apply ethical and sustainable practices in fashion supply chain.
  • CO3 - Appreciate use of Green materials in garment manufacturing.
  • CO4 - Interpret fashion needs and consumption.
  • CO5 - Use ethical and sustainable practices in garment production.
