I. RATIONALE

The fashion styling course is intended to provide an emerging career option, driven by the rapid growth of the fashion and film industries. The demand for fashion stylists is rising, as they create complete looks based on themes, events, personal style, and current trends. The course provide the student with skills encompassing designing, commercial styling, personal shopping, image makeovers, and collaboration with magazines, brands, and production houses to shape new fashion trends in the fast moving fashion industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry/employer expected outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Apply Design and Commercial styling techniques enabling collaboration with fashion stakeholders for various fashion trends.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop garments with comprehensive and cohesive looks tailored to various themes, events, and individual personal styles, reflecting current fashion trends.
  • CO2 - Apply Design and Commercial styling techniques enabling collaboration with magazines, brands, and production houses to produce fashion-forward images and trends.
  • CO3 - Apply techniques in Personal Shopping and Image Makeovers to suit the clients needs.
  • CO4 - Analyze the demand and supply in the fashion and film industries with respect to skilled fashion stylists and emerging career opportunities.
  • CO5 - Suggest strategies for collaboration with Fashion Stakeholders: including photographers, makeup artists, and designers in order to create impactful fashion images and contribute to the development of new fashion trends.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Create a mood board to inspire a new fashion collection. Select a theme or inspiration (e.g., vintage, futuristic, nature). Gather images, fabrics, colors, and textures that reflect the theme. Arrange the elements into a cohesive mood board. ( Present and explain the mood board to the class).
  • Develop a versatile capsule wardrobe for a specific lifestyle (e.g., corporate, casual, travel). Choose 10-15 essential clothing items that can be mixed and matched. Create outfit combinations and provide visual examples.
  • Enhance an outfit using different accessories. Style the outfit using various accessories (jewelry, bags, scarves, hats). Provide before and after images to showcase the transformation.
  • Document and analyze street fashion trends in a specific location.( Choose a popular location known for its fashion). Take photos of diverse street styles. Identify and describe common trends and unique styles observed. Compile the findings into a visual report.

  • Explore, research and present case studies of three fashion styling brands that focus on sustainability.
