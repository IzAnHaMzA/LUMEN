I. RATIONALE

International Fashion Business focuses on the strategic and entrepreneurial aspects of fashion brands. This course intends to give the students an overview of branding, budgeting, distribution, finance, forecasting, marketing, merchandising, and retail operations in the global fashion market. This curriculum delves into creating and managing fashion-related information across diverse cultures and markets worldwide.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences: Apply the concepts of global trade to forecasting fashion in global and domestic markets.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify various global fashion trends and advances in international fashion businesses.
  • CO2 - Analyze the regulatory frameworks and international agreements affecting fashion trade.
  • CO3 - Formulate comprehensive international marketing plans, incorporating market research, brand positioning, digital marketing, and cultural considerations.
  • CO4 - Develop strategies for participating in International Trade Fairs.
  • CO5 - Apply Trend Analysis and Forecasting Techniques to predict future international fashion trends and effectively manage luxury fashion brands.
  • CO6 - Conduct comprehensive financial analyses of fashion businesses.
