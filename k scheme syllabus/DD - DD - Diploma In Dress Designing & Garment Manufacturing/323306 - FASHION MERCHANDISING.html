I. RATIONALE

Fashion is a an extremely dynamic & multifaceted area of business and fashion merchandising is a job that is done worldwide & is a career opportunity for Diploma students of Dress Designing & Garment Manufacturing. Fashion merchandisers work with designers to ensure that the designs will be affordable and desired by target market by following the five rights of merchandising that meet the need of consumers thus turning a profit. This course empowers students with skills of merchandising in clothing Industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry identified competency through various teaching learning experiences: Use relevant merchandising principles and procedure in garment designing & manufacturing.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the environmental factors influencing the fashion Cycle &Principles of Fashion.
  • CO2 - Apply the theories of Fashion adoption.
  • CO3 - Analyze the role of Fashion Players in market.
  • CO4 - Classify the types of merchandiser their principles and functions.
  • CO5 - Identify the role of merchandiser in Apparel Export House.
