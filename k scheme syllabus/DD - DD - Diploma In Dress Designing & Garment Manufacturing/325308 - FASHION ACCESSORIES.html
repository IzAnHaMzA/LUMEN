I. RATIONALE

This course provides an engaging exploration of fashion accessories, including headgear, handbags, belts, and jewellery. Students will study both Indian and Western styles, gaining insights into their history, materials, and construction techniques. Accessories are crucial for enhancing personal style, adding glamour and uniqueness to appearances. The fashion industry’s growth is closely linked to the evolution of accessories, underscoring their essential role. This knowledge is invaluable for aspiring fashion professionals, preparing them to make a significant impact in the industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help students achieve industry-expected outcomes - "Apply various Indian craft practices to develop fashion accessories with respect to end uses and revival of crafts through entrepreneurial activities".
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Analyze various fashion accessory designs by considering the impact of cultural and societal factors.
  • CO2 - Create designs of headgear, Handbags, belts based on distinct themes using various materials.
  • CO3 - Make headgear, handbags, belts by applying different techniques of construction.
  • CO4 - Identify jewellery design principles, materials, and techniques to create innovative pieces.
  • CO5 - Organize exhibitions of mentioned accessories for promotional activities.
