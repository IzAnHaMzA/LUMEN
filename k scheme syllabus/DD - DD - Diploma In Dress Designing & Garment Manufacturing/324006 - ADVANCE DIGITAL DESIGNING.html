I. RATIONALE

This course introduces students to Create and alter true-to-life 3D garments, it reduces sample production, shipment, and material waste. Digital designing is empowered with virtual sampling in shorter time. This course gives skills about accurately transferring visual and silhouette of designs as fast as one can imagine them. It is Sustainable way of production in significant cost saving. This course also provides opportunity to learn skills of digital embroidery used in apparel industry at mass level. digitized embroidery empower to create high-quality embroidery designs, with cutting-edge digitizing and editing capabilities.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the students to attain the following industry/ employer expected outcome- Use designing and embroidery softwares used in apparel industries.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply various designing software in garment industries.
  • CO2 - Use various simulation techniques.
  • CO3 - Apply types of trimmings to various garments.
  • CO4 - Apply 3D Techniques for virtual fashion presentation.
  • CO5 - Create digital embroidery designs using various embroidery softwares.
  • CO6 - Use various embroidery designs for making various patterns.
