I. RATIONALE

This course provide 2D product Visualization in a fashion designing & recreates the fashion design production process in an entirely digital environment it empowers the students to go to market with their digitally designed products . The Fashion Design Software shortens product development cycle times by enabling design teams to use tools to create designs easily and fast, and enhances business velocity by enabling collaboration between design, marketing and production.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student attain the following industry-identified competencies through various teaching and learning experiences: Apply Digital designing & Drafting Marker planning ,grading techniques to the fashion industries..
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify different tools & application for 2D Illustration.
  • CO2 - Apply E- style, E- Photo studio for achieving, 2D effects on the apparel.
  • CO3 - Create technical flats and technical pack for various design.
  • CO4 - Create various texture & textile prints in fashion Studio.
  • CO5 - Identify the use of cut planner, style manager in fashion designing.
