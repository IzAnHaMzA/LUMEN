I. RATIONALE

In fashion world fashion portfolio plays an important role for presenting various garment lines. This course provides the visualization of fashion portfolio ideas from boards to final designs. It develops the skills of theme based designing, fashion garments designing and accessories designing for the client in fashion industry. It also provides the skills required for presentation techniques for fashion brands.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences: Design contemporary garments line applying technical aspects and design boards.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Design sequential fashion portfolios using theme base designs and boards.
  • CO2 - Use recent trends in fashion for design development processes.
  • CO3 - Create various garment lines based on various design boards.
  • CO4 - Design portfolios with illustrations applying creativity and innovation.
  • CO5 - Showcase various theme based collections deploying creative presentation techniques.
