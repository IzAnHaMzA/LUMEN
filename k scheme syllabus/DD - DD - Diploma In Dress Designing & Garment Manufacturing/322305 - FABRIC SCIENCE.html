I. RATIONALE

Fabric is foundationally important in creating silhouette and aesthetics. The structure of a textile, woven or knitted, comprises unique qualities that will help designers, merchandisers to choose appropriate fabrics for their designs, and help to determine the shape of the final design. Fabric Science course helps to understand complex structures of fabrics, multiple textures of yarn, several colors, to form a dense, sumptuous material that falls in dramatic folds. It renders the fabric attractive and better resistant to laundering and wear.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Student will be able to acheive and demonstrate the following CO on completion of Course based learning :- Select different types of functional & value added fabrics for relevant end use.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select various woven fabrics as per the client needs.
  • CO2 - Select the basic weaves of woven fabrics according to various end-use.
  • CO3 - Interpret Knit & Knitted fabric for comfort and versatility & their performance.
  • CO4 - Select the nonwoven fabrics on the basis of performance and end-use.
  • CO5 - Use of textile pretreatment processes sustainably for grey fabric.
  • CO6 - Apply the finishes as per client requirements.
