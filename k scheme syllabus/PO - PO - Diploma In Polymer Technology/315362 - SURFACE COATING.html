I. RATIONALE

Surface Coating Technology is a critical discipline in polymer technology, encompassing the study and application of various coating techniques to enhance polymer products' performance, aesthetics, and durability. Surface coatings significantly improve polymer products' physical, chemical, and mechanical properties. These enhancements include increased resistance to wear, corrosion, and UV radiation, which extend the lifespan and functionality of the products This course is designed for diploma students in polymer technology to equip them with the theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary to understand and apply surface coating processes.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
• Apply different coating materials as per the given application.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply the selection criteria to choose suitable pigments for an exterior paint.
  • CO2 - Implement safety measures for handling and storing paint in a laboratory/industry
  • CO3 - Use relevant paint application methods for specific applications.
  • CO4 - Test the paint as per standard specification.
  • CO5 - Combine polymer products using appropriate adhesives.
