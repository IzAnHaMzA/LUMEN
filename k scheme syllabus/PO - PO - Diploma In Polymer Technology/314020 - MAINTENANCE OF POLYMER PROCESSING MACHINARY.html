I. RATIONALE

This course is intended to create awareness about routine, preventive, online and breakdown maintenance of various polymers processing machines. Polymers diploma engineer (also called technologist) are supposed to work on various polymer processing machines. These machines must be maintained properly by routine, preventive and breakdown maintenance in order to achieve steady and long life performance. In small scale industries maintenance of machines is assigned to production technicians

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Maintain polymer processing machines and auxiliary equipments.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Implement preventive maintenance techniques to reduce breakdowns and failures
  • CO2 - Diagnose and repair the electrical faults of polymer processing machines
  • CO3 - Perform small repairs on the polymer processing machines to rectify mechanical faults.
  • CO4 - Troubleshoot the common faults in a polymer processing machines.
  • CO5 - Maintain record of preventive and breakdown maintenance.
