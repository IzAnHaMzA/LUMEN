I. RATIONALE

Students with a Diploma in polymer technology must work with various polymer Moulding techniques. This course focuses on transforming polymer materials into valuable end products through diverse methods. After completing this course, students will acquire the knowledge, and hands-on skill on various moulding techniques, encompassing machines, auxiliary equipment, process optimization, and troubleshooting for each method.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help the student attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: • Operate various moulding machines and troubleshoot various polymer moulding Products.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Operate Compression moulding Machines by various process parameters
  • CO2 - Optimize transfer molding parameters
  • CO3 - Proposed the remedies of various defects by analyzing injection moulding process
  • CO4 - Use appropriate rotational moulding process for preparing articles.
  • CO5 - Evaluate factors that affect thermoforming process, such as air, temperature, mold temp

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Moulding Temperature: Make a chart of Moulding and processing temperature (Minimum 20 polymer) for Commodity, Engineering, Specialty, and thermosetting material, also prepare the presentation
  • Explore and create a presentation/ report on Flash Type, Positive, and Semipositive types of moulds used in compression moulding by Including detailed diagrams and examples of products that are typically produced using each type of mould also Highlight the advantages and limitations of each mould type.
  • Raw Material Analysis: Collect the information of raw material (specification, MFI, Grade, COA) name of manufacturer, supplier, prepare a report for Commodity and Engg. Material and make a presentation in class
  • Trouble shooting of article: Collect different household article and prepare chart on common faults occurred in Injection, Compression, Thermoforming and rotation molding
  • Application of Machine: Collect the information of available articles, prepare a chart of the product based on process (injection, Compression, Transfer, Rotation, Thermoforming)
    • Assignment
  • Prepare standard operating process and molding cycle of compression moulding in a form of Visual Presentation and present the findings in the class.
  • Prepare flow chart for work Instruction for Hand Operated injection Moulding
  • List the Safety equipment’s used to operate the various polymer moulding machines, Explain importance of personal protective equipments
  • Collect the process parameter used in Pot and Plunger type machine, with respect to different material (UF, MF, PF)
  • Prepare the chart for processing variable and its effect on various process like compression, Transfer, injection and rotational moulding
  • List the Equipment manufacture from Thermoforming and Rotational Moulding, and search the information on Rotational Machine and Thermoforming machine Manufacturers.
