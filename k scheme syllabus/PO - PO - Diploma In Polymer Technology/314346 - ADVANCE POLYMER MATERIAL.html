I. RATIONALE

There is an increasing number of diverse and demanding applications of polymer materials, and there is a need to use advanced or unique polymers for specific applications. The non-biodegradable properties of polymers are a major concern with respect to the environmental aspect, so it has become mandatory to introduce biodegradable polymers for newer applications. The increasing demand for new engineering and specialty materials in automobile, space, medical, and other fields has led to development of new polymer materials and polymer blends.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The course aims to characterize and select advanced polymeric materials for high-end applications.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Choose appropriate high-performance polymers for specific applications.
  • CO2 - Determine the specialty polymers based on properties and applications.
  • CO3 - Use biodegradable polymers in designing eco-friendly products.
  • CO4 - Apply knowledge of polymers to enhance properties of various polymers for specific applications.
  • CO5 - Select suitable thermoplastic elastomer for Specific application.
