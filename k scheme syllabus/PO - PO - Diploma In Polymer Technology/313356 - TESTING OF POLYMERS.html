I. RATIONALE

The course on testing of polymer for diploma engineers provide comprehensive knowledge of methods and techniques employed evaluating the properties and performance of polymers/products. This course aims to equip the students with necessary skills of testing polymer products by using standard testing procedure, significance and interpretation of test results

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Student will be able to Test various raw materials and polymer products
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select relevant standard for testing products
  • CO2 - Perform relevant mechanical test for assuring quality of product
  • CO3 - Test thermal and rheological property of plastic for assuring quality of product
  • CO4 - Perform relevant electrical and Optical test for assuring quality of product
  • CO5 - Apply chemical and weathering property test for relevant applications

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Troubleshooting: Student should repair non-working testing equipment’s in laboratories
  • LLL: Download polymer testing videos. Give class presentation
  • Testing and analysis of material: Identify the grades of material by MFI test. Prepare quality reports in standard formats and submit it to project guide
  • Product Analysis: Compare properties of two different products from different sources, make a report and submit it to project guide
  • Material Identification: Test different plastic materials commonly used in society. Prepare a technical report and submit it to concerned project guide.
  • Sample Preparation: Prepare samples for different test, collect standard operating procedure for preparing samples and tests
  • IS code: Update latest amendments of IS related to polymer products and present to industry and consumer association
  • Testing machine making/repairing : Make testing equipment (for compression test, resilience test, abrasion resistance, impact tester, constant temperature bath etc)
  • Explore and compile information in the form of a report on standard-producing organizations such as ASTM, ISO, IS, and BIS, their roles, functions, and development standards
  • Develop a case study by Investigating real-world applications of destructive and nondestructive testing methods. Provide examples of where each type is used and analyze the advantages and disadvantages of each
    • Assignment
  • Collect IS standards for testing of polymer products. (pipe, container, bottles, films, sheets) and make its presentation and present it in the class
  • Assist in consultancy and testing work of department
  • Gather information of polymer manufacturers and suppliers in team (grades, rates, minimum quantity, bulk discounts, taxes, delivery period etc.)
  • Visit an existing testing center and collect information of registration procedure, plant layout, machinery specifications and price, electrical load, manpower requirement, rate list, annual turnover, major clients and terms and conditions. Make a report of it
  • Write an essay comparing destructive and nondestructive testing methods. Include examples, advantages, and limitations of each type and present the findings in the class
  • Develop a case study report presenting the material, testing methods, and implications of physical properties on material selection
    •
