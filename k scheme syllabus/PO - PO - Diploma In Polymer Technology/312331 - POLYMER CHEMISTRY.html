I. RATIONALE

Today polymer materials are very important, and one cannot imagine life without it. Polymers are being used in all applications like automobile, construction, medical, sports and in space moreover continuous research is going in the field of polymers. This course enables the polymer technology students to use and apply, various fundamental knowledge of polymer chemistry like concept of Polymer, classification of polymers, Polymerization mechanism, Molecular weight and its determination, relationship between structure and properties and degradation mechanism. This proficiency will be necessary for learning higher semester courses like Plastic material, blends and composites, processing and testing of plastics.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The student will be able to apply fundamental knowledge of polymer chemistry for selection of various polymers for processing plants.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Classify Polymers
  • CO2 - Choose the proper polymerization technique to manufacture polymer.
  • CO3 - Interpret the relation between structure and properties of polymers.
  • CO4 - Select appropriate method for determination of molecular weight of polymers .
  • CO5 - Identify different types of degradation of polymers and its prevention.
