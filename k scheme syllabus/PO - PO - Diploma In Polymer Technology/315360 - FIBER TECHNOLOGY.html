I. RATIONALE

Industrial applications of polymers in the form of fiber like rope, fabrics are increasing day by day in todays scenario. This course is developed to acquaint Polymer engineering technocrats about types, manufacturing techniques and applications of synthetic fibers. The application of this course is widely found in textile manufacturing, Polymer packaging, advertising and media industries.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Select relevant technologies for manufacturing of different types of fibers.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Differentiate between natural and synthetic fibers based on their chemical composition and properties.
  • CO2 - Use melt spinning technique and post spinning operation to make fibre.
  • CO3 - Use solution spinning technique and post spinning operation to make fibre.
  • CO4 - Apply knowledge of fiber properties to suggest appropriate uses for polyester, nylon, polypropylene, and acrylic fibers.
  • CO5 - Select suitable high performance fibre for industrial application.
