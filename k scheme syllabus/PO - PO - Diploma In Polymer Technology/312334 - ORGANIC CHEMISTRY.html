I. RATIONALE

Diploma polymer technologists have to deal with various polymeric materials. This course develops the concepts and principles of organic chemistry such as organic compounds, their preparation, properties and uses. The course will also help to understand organic reactions in polymer and polymer manufacturing processes. It lays foundation for future courses in higher semesters.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help the students attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: • Apply principles of organic chemistry to solve polymer engineering-related problems.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use basic concept of various organic compounds in polymer synthesis.
  • CO2 - Select appropriate Aromatic compounds in polymer manufacturing process.
  • CO3 - Apply different types of functional group in synthesis of polymer.
  • CO4 - Select relevant organic compound based upon its stereoregularity in polymer synthesis.
  • CO5 - Apply the purification process of monomer for polymer synthesis.
