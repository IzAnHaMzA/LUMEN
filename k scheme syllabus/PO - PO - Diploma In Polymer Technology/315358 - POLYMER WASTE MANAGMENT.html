I. RATIONALE

Polymers have wide applications and advantages, and their products are used almost everywhere, which results in the generation of polymer wastes that cause environmental pollution as they are non-biodegradable. This course boosts an idea of recycling and reusing polymer waste. Recycling of waste polymers has a broader scope; hence, this course will be of importance in industries as well as to students who wish to become entrepreneurs by establishing polymer waste recycling units.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use relevant polymer waste recycling techniques to conserve and reduce pollution for a sustainable environment
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply the concept of polymer waste management to categorize the sources of polymer waste
  • CO2 - Select suitable methods and standards for managing polymer waste.
  • CO3 - Use appropriate sorting and separation techniques to control polymer waste
  • CO4 - Utilize appropriate recycling techniques to manage polymer waste
  • CO5 - Evaluate suitable polymer waste for manufacturing of recycled products

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Assignment
  • Write an essay on the importance of waste management in modern society
  • Prepare flow chart of polymer waste from generation to disposal
  • Prepare chart of steps in polymer waste recycling process
  • Gather information of polymer waste collection agencies and recycling industries
  • Visit an existing recycling unit/industry and collect information of registration procedure, plant layout, machinery specifications and price, electrical load, manpower requirement, rate list, annual turnover, major clients and terms and conditions. Make a report of it
  • Develop a poster campaign to raise awareness about the need for polymer waste management
  • Develop an action plan for reducing pollution in your Institute/local community
  • Describe the gasification process and its role in recovering gas from polymer waste and Compare the environmental impact of gasification with other waste management methods
  • Conduct a case study on a company that successfully uses recycled PET
    • Micro project
  • Collect the polymer waste from houses, shops and sort them according to their size, color, codes and shape and prepare a report
  • Visit the Pyrolysis plant of Municipal Corporation and prepare a report of mixed waste polymer to produce fuel
  • Explore and define the 4 R's in the context of polymer waste management.Create a poster or infographic explaining each concept with examples.Develop a small project or initiative that applies the 4 R's in a practical setting
  • Choose one type of recycled polymer and create a product using recycled material. Document the process and present your findings on the feasibility and quality of the product
  • Depolymerize the collected waste and recover monomer and measure quantity of product
  • Preparea technical report by conducting a small-scale pyrolysis or gasification experiment using polymer waste and analyze the products obtained
  • Collect various plastic waste materials and perform granulation. Create a detailed report on the effectiveness of the granulation process and suggest improvements
  • Create a flowchart for each tertiary recycling method and demonstrate one of the processes using a small-scale experiment
  • Collect the information of manufacturer of recycling machines in India and abroad and prepare a power point presentation and present in classroom
