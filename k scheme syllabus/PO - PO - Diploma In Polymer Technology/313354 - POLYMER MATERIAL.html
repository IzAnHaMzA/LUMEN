I. RATIONALE

Diverse polymer materials are used for various applications in plastic industry. The course summarizes the preparation, properties and applications of thermoplastics and thermosetting materials and will also be beneficial to the students of diploma in polymer technology interested to become entrepreneurs by establishing raw materials and additives material supply firms

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Student is able to choose relevant polymer materials for appropriate polymer products
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Choose relevant polymer materials for manufacturing commodity products
  • CO2 - Select relevant polymer materials for manufacturing engineering products
  • CO3 - Explain preparation of polymer materials for manufacturing of electrical product
  • CO4 - Use relevant additives for processing of polymer materials
  • CO5 - Deploy relevant compounding equipment for polymer compounding

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Raw Material: Collect information (specifications, rates, terms and conditions, discounts, addresses) of raw materials manufacturers and suppliers of any two polymer materials manufacturing. Make class presentation
  • Material Preparation: Prepare any two thermosetting materials in the laboratory
  • Flow sheet: Prepare flow sheet for manufacturing of any two polymers
  • Model making: Prepare models of structure of polymers. (at least five polymers)
  • Report writing: Prepare a report on applications of engineering polymers by using internet and journals
    • Assignment
  • Collect samples of thermosetting polymer materials from surrounding places and make a chart with their properties and applications
  • Prepare a chart for abbreviations for polymer materials
  • Gather the information of polymer materials manufacturer and suppliers (grades, rates, minimum quantity, bulk discounts, taxes, delivery period etc.).
  • Visit an existing polymer material supplier/shop and collect information of registration procedure, plant layout, material data sheet, order size, commissions, manufacturer's details and price, manpower requirement, rate list, annual turnover, major clients and terms and conditions. Make a report of it
  • Collect information of different engineering polymer materials used in automobile industries (type, prosperities, and end application)
