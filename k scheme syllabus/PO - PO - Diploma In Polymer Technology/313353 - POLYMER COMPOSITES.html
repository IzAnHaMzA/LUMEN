I. RATIONALE

The demand for lightweight, durable, and high-performance materials has surged in the ever-evolving landscape of polymer engineering. Polymer composites, are a revolutionary class of materials, have emerged as a key player in meeting the demands across various industries. After completion of this course enhances career prospects, sought-after in manufacturing, application of composites and quality control.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: • Manufacture of various polymer composite products.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply the knowledge of polymer composites for various applications.
  • CO2 - Choose relevant polymer matrix for given application of composites.
  • CO3 - Use suitable reinforcement for specified application of composites.
  • CO4 - Prepare polymer composite using relevant processing technique.
  • CO5 - Uses Nano, Bio-polymer composite for different applications.

Micro project
  • Manufacturing: Prepare laminate at room temperature and elevated temperature. Identify and apply best practices for disposal of waste material for environmental sustainability.
  • b. Reinforcement: Prepare laminate by varying the proportion of resin and reinforcement. Identify and apply bet practices for disposal of resins and wastes for environmental sustainability.
  • c. Laminates: Test the mechanical properties of laminate. Prepare a technical report to be submitted along with standard operation procedures.
  • d. Processing Techniques: analyze composite processing techniques (download PPT/videos for best practices) with special reference to environment sustainability and give class presentation
    • Assignment
  • a. Collect information of industries manufacturing glass fibers. Prepare a report.
