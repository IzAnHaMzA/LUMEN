I. RATIONALE

Polymer processing is a core skill for diploma students in Polymer Technology, as it enables them to transform raw materials into useful and innovative products. Polymer processing covers various methods of shaping, molding, extruding, and forming polymers into desired shapes and sizes, such as films, fibers, pipes, containers, etc. Polymer processing is a dynamic and evolving field that offers many opportunities for innovation and entrepreneurship, as well as addressing environmental and social challenges through sustainable and biodegradable polymer products.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
• Operate various types of polymer processing machines.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use the extrusion principle to design simple products.
  • CO2 - Choose the appropriate type of blow moulding for a given application.
  • CO3 - Operate Calendaring machine for manufacturing of PVC sheet / film.
  • CO4 - Use appropriate techniques to manufacture various types of foams for specific applications.
  • CO5 - Use appropriate joining and welding techniques to prepare polymer products
