I. RATIONALE

Elastomers are fundamental to the automotive, aerospace, medical, and consumer goods industries. The Elastomers Technology course for the diploma engineers in polymer technology is designed to offer students with the necessary skills and knowledge that are crucial for designing and engineering elastomeric materials with relevant properties.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Manufacture rubber products of different rubbers and quality control (test method) of rubber products used in different applications.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use thermoplastic and thermosetting rubbers in relevant industries.
  • CO2 - Select appropriate synthetic rubber for specific applications.
  • CO3 - Choose suitable compounding methods and ingredients for product formulations.
  • CO4 - Apply the relevant vulcanization systems for the manufacture of rubber products.
  • CO5 - Apply suitable test methods to test the properties of various elastomeric materials.
