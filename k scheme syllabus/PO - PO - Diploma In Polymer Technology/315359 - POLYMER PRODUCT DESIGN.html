I. RATIONALE

Plastic products are used in many applications, replacing conventional materials faster. As per consumer needs, there is a need to change/modify the product's design and design new products to improve aesthetics and performance. This course also imparts essential skills and compressive knowledge of polymer materials, processing techniques, and design principles for creating innovative plastic products. It emphasizes sustainable practices and material selection, enabling students to design cost-effective and environmentally friendly products.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: • Design plastic products for desired applications.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Evaluate various types of plastics for particular application.
  • CO2 - Apply the knowledge of basic design principles to design a simple/commercial plastic product.
  • CO3 - Examine appropriate product design concepts for specialized plastic product.
  • CO4 - Select a suitable plastic processing method according to a product's design, shape, and size.
  • CO5 - Interpret plastic product design concept with respective specific application.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Prepare a detailed report by applying plastic product design to modular plastic toys that can be assembled in various configurations.
  • Prepare a detailed presentation collecting the plastic bottles of at least three commercial suppliers and compare plastic product designs and each product.
  • Apply plastic product design concept for a plastic gear for a small mechanical device.
  • Apply plastic product design concept for a plastic container with an integrated hinged lid for food storage.
  • Select one of the composite products listed in your curriculum and develop a detailed process plan for its manufacturing. Include material selection, design considerations, and process steps.
  • Choose any one product from commodity, medical, engineering, electrical, engineering, automobile, and home appliances products and create a detailed report on its design, material selection, and manufacturing process. Include sketches, material properties, and justifications for the chosen method.
  • Design a product using thermoplastic or thermoset materials and create a prototype using a selected manufacturing process. Document the steps and considerations involved.
    • Assignment
  • Choose a plastic product and analyze the criteria considered in selecting its manufacturing process. Present your findings.
  • Explain the different methods used to manufacture hollow plastic products and Compare and contrast these methods in terms of efficiency, Cost, and product quality.
  • Outline the steps in selecting the process for manufacturing a composite automobile panel, considering weight, strength, and aesthetics.
  • Explain the materials and processes used to produce plastic bottles and chairs. Discuss the factors influencing material and process selection for these products.
  • Identify and explain at least five criteria used to select plastic manufacturing processes. Discuss how each criterion affects the decision-making process in a manufacturing setup.
