I. RATIONALE

Effective road safety and management are crucial for reducing traffic accidents, savings lives, and minimizing injuries. implementing various measures such as improved road design, enforcement of traffic laws, and public awareness campaigns can significantly decrease the likelihood of accidents. Efficient road management also ensures smoother traffic flow, reducing congestion and environmental impact. This leads to safer, more reliable, and environmentally sustainable transportation systems. This course aims at to impart required knowledge of road safety measures to the diploma students so that likelihood of road accidents can be minimized.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Minimize the road accidents & fatalities.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Analyze road accident characteristics in a given local region.
  • CO2 - Classify road junctions based on its geometric features.
  • CO3 - Identify different components of a given pathways for universal accessibility.
  • CO4 - Draw different types of parking spaces.
  • CO5 - Enlist traffic signals, signs, rules and regulations adopted in our country.
  • CO6 - Identify road safety measures during a given event.
