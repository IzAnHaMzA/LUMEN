I. RATIONALE

In the textile processing industry, raw fabric is converted into finished products using various chemical methods. The effectiveness of these processes is influenced by the arrangement of machines and the movement of materials. Efficient use of space and equipment boosts performance, while careful management of water, energy, and chemicals lowers production costs. This course provides diploma engineers with foundational knowledge in textile process management, focusing on optimizing space, machines, and resources to enhance production efficiency and tackle industry challenges.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply management principles to oversee textile processing operations effectively.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Develop layout plan for various textile processing units.
  • CO2 - Calculate production for the given textile processing machine based on parameters.
  • CO3 - Calculate water consumption in the given textile process and machine based on parameters.
  • CO4 - Calculate energy consumption in the given textile process and machine based on parameters.
  • CO5 - Calculate chemical consumption in the given textile process and machine based on parameters.
  • CO6 - Select relevant material handling equipment in the textile processing.

Micro project
  • Visit a local industry. analyze accident possibility in the industry. Suggest precaution measure for the accidents. prepare a report.
  • Visit a local industry and study the chemical used in industry. Suggest the alternative chemical. Prepare a report on the data.
  • Visit a local industry and create a material flow chart. Propose an improved version to save time, with an explanation.
  • Visit a local industry and document the different water sources used.
  • Visit a local industry. Study the production norms. Prepare the production report on the given production norms.
  • Visit a local industry. Study the energy sources used in industry. Suggest the alternative sources. Prepare a report on the data.
  • Visit a local industry, Study the time required for material handling for each process. Suggest various material handling machine.
  • Visit a local industry. Analyze the plant layout. Design a new layout with minimum defect.
