I. RATIONALE

In textile industry, various processes such as pretreatment, dyeing, printing, and finishing are used to produce quality textile products, which improve the aesthetic and the market value of the textile produced. To maintain the textile quality and efficiency of the production machines, process control and quality control are important. Number of tests are performed at various production stages to check and maintain the quality as per the customer requirements. The knowledge and skills related to the process control and quality control are essential for the diploma engineer to produce quality textile. This course will help the diploma engineer to produce quality textile and solve broad based problems in the textile processing.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use process control and quality control principles to produce quality textile.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select relevant process control parameters for mechanical pretreatment.
  • CO2 - Select relevant process control parameters and test methods for quality control of chemical pretreatment.
  • CO3 - Select relevant process control parameters for dyeing machines.
  • CO4 - Select relevant process control parameters and test methods for quality control of printing process.
  • CO5 - Select relevant process control parameters and test methods for quality control of finishing process.
