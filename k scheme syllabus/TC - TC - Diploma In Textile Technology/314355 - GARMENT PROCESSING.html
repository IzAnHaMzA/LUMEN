I. RATIONALE

In textile industry, varieties of garment undergo various chemical processes which improve the aesthetic properties and market value of the garment. To achieve the high quality of garments, the diploma engineers must have adequate knowledge and skills related to garment processing machines, relevant finishing methods, garment printing techniques, fabric care treatments, and so on. This course will prepare the diploma engineers to process different garments and reproduce desired effects and finishes. It will also help the diploma engineers to analyze and rectify broad based problems in the garment processing.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply wet processing principles in the coloration and finishing of the given garment.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use relevant wet processing sequence for the given garment.
  • CO2 - Use relevant machines and methods for coloration of the given garment.
  • CO3 - Select relevant finish type for finishing the given garment.
  • CO4 - Create different washing effects on the given denims and non-denims.
  • CO5 - Select relevant stain removing and dry-cleaning treatment for the given garment.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Collection of care label samples: Visit garment processing/ manufacturing units and collect at least 30 samples of various care labels. Classify them according to types and present the results.
  • Denim wash by using varying oxidizing agents:Select 4 oxidising agents and Prepare atleast 20 samples by optimising concentration of oxidising agent,time of treatment.
  • Comparison of conventional & digital printing of garments: Collect information from various sources to prepare a comparative chart related to type of machine, machine cost, operational cost, dyes & chemicals needed for garment printing. Present the results.
  • Short film on working of machine: Visit different garment processing industries. Observe the working operations of machines at various stages and capture video clips. Edit the video clips as per specified process sequence and make a short film. Present the short film.

  • Prepare Computer-based presentation on different garment printing techniques incorporating relevant visuals, videos and animations.
  • Prepare a comparative chart of the colour fastness of various dyed garment samples.
  • Do internet survey to find out new developments in garment processing.
  • Collect samples of different garment printing techniques.
