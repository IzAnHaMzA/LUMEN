I. RATIONALE

In todays context, the success of the various businesses depends on the efficient logistic and supply chain management at various stages of business. The basic knowledge of this area for the diploma engineers is very essential to work effectively in the textile industry. This course will make the diploma engineers to apply the principles of logistics and supply chain in handling different types of textile materials and they will be able to solve broad-based logistics and supply chain related problems.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Students will be able to manage logistics and supply chain management of textile materials.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply basic principles of supply chain management in textile industry.
  • CO2 - Implement supply chain process effectively in the textile industry.
  • CO3 - Apply principles of logistic management in the textile industry.
  • CO4 - Make decision for sourcing and outsourcing of textile material based on the given criteria.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Assignment
  • Access websites of two textile companies, search for the supplier registration forms. Compare the formats and prepare a registration process flow chart. submit the report.
  • Visit nearby textile industry warehouse. Prepare its layout plan and flowchart of material movement. Submit your report.
  • Visit nearby garment unit and plan supply chain network for the shirts. Submit your report.
  • Explore websites of textile firms utilizing RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) technology and examine its application within their operations.
  • Access the websites of three apparel retail companies and examine their supplier selection process based on specified criteria. Afterwards, compile a report summarizing your findings.
    • Micro project
  • Visit any apparel retail store and trace the supply chain of apparel retail store. Draw supply chain chart. Discuss with store manager about issues faced in supply chain management. Present the report.
  • Visit purchase department of two textile companies nearby and collect documents such as formats of Requisition, Quotation, Comparative Statement, Purchase Order, Delivery Challan, Invoice etc. Compare the formats and present report.
  • Visit purchase department of nearby Textile Company and study various steps involved in purchasing process.
  • Visit a nearby textile company and analyze exemplary approaches in logistics management. Afterwards, submit a comprehensive report detailing your findings.
  • Visit three apparel manufacturing companies and conduct an analysis of their inventory control and replenishment strategies.
