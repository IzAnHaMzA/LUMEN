I. RATIONALE

In textile industry, various chemical processes are carried out on variety of textile substrates. The chemical processing of textile is a value addition process by way of enhancing aesthetic properties through printing and other processes. To achieve the high quality prints, the diploma engineer must have adequate knowledge and skills related to natural substrates, various dyes, chemicals, relevant auxiliaries, various printing styles and printing methodology for different fabrics. The knowledge and skills gained will help them to solve broad based problems in the textile industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use relevant coloring matters, ingredients, printing processes and equipment for printing of natural fabrics.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select relevant thickeners and ingredients for printing the given natural substrate.
  • CO2 - Use relevant printing method and style for the given natural substrate.
  • CO3 - Use relevant machine for fabric printing as per design requirements and production capacity.
  • CO4 - Develop print on the given cotton fabric using relevant dyes/ pigments.
  • CO5 - Develop print on the given wool and silk fabric using relevant dyes/ pigments.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Assignment
  • Prepare chart of different chemicals used in printing of cotton fabric and their role in printing process.
  • Collect information about novel printing techniques being used in different industries. Prepare a comparative chart and submit.
  • Collect information from screen manufacturing units about type of fabric, photo sensitive material used used for screen manufacturing.
    • Micro project
  • Evaluate fastness properties of printed fabric: Collect at least 10 different printed fabric samples from industry. Evaluate their wash fastness, rubbing fastness and light fastness properties using specified ISO tests. Present the results with end use of the tested fabric samples.
  • Preparation of a short film on working of machine: Visit different textile printing industries. Observe the working operations of machine at various stages and capture those processes using video/ mobile camera with permission from the industry. Edit the videos as per specified process sequence and make a short film. Present the short film.
