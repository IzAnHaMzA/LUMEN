I. RATIONALE

In the textile industry, various chemicals are used along with water in different processes. To maintain the quality of products at relevant stages of the process, measurement of various water quality parameters, selection of fuel, and analysis of chemicals and auxiliaries are required. This course covers essential testing methods for chemicals and auxiliaries in the textile industry. This course will help the diploma engineer to apply industrial chemistry principles to maintain product quality and employ analytical techniques for wet processing.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply chemistry principles for textile-related industrial applications.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Measure quality parameters of water used in wet processing.
  • CO2 - Select relevant fuel for boiler used in wet processing.
  • CO3 - Select relevant chemicals and co-ordination compounds used in textile industry.
  • CO4 - Select relevant oil, Soap, and Detergent for wet processing.
  • CO5 - Estimate the purity of chemicals used in textile industry.
