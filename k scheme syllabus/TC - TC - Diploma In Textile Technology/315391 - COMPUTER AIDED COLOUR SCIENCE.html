I. RATIONALE

In textile industry, various processes such as dyeing, printing, and finishing are used to manufacture quality textile. These major processes improve the aesthetic as well as the market value of the textile. Dyeing and printing are processes used for colouration of textile substrates such as cellulosic, polyester, nylon, acrylic and their blends. The knowledge and skills related to light theory, perception of colour, colour mixing using computer based colour matching system is essential for the diploma engineer to create quality textile with optimum cost. This course is developed in such a way that basic concepts and principles of light theory, colour mixing theories and applications of computer colour matching system will help the diploma engineer to get quality fabrics with optimum cost and solve broad based problems in the textile colouration processes.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Prepare colour recipe in wet processing using principles of colour science and computer colour matching system.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use relevant spectrophotometer for colour measurement in textiles.
  • CO2 - Use relevant technique of colour specification in colour matching system.
  • CO3 - Use relevant colour standards in colour matching systems.
  • CO4 - Use relevant procedure to formulate quality recipe for colouration.
  • CO5 - Use relevant application to identify the given colour properties.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Shade matching using CCM: Collect different standard samples from different industries and reproduce any one on given substrate in laboratory using recipe formulation application of computer colour matching.
  • Preparation of Database: Prepare database of given dye on given substrate and evaluate K/S for its applicability on computer colour matching system.
  • Importance of colour matching: Collect various standard samples and batch samples of textile substrate and use colour difference application to analyze accuracy of matching.
  • Study of Metamerism: Collect different commercially dyed textile substrate and evaluate metameric index using computer colour matching system and find better recipe by using recipe formulation application.
  • CCM systems used in industries: Collect information from different manufacturers of spectrophotometers used in CCM and compare their working, structure and techno commercial importance.
    • Assignment
  • Challanges in shade matching using CCM: Visit to nearby textile industries and find challenges in shade matching using CCM
  • Spectrophotometers in textile industries: Collect information from internet about spectrophotometers used in textile industry and compare their working, structure and techno commercial importance and prepare a report
  • Tolarance limits in colour differences: Visit Nearby textile industries and collect tolerance limit practices in shade matching and present report
