I. RATIONALE

Advancement taking place in chemical processing of textiles are in accordance with making use of minimum water for processing; legislation on health, safety and environment. To achieve the quality textiles, the diploma engineers must have adequate knowledge of new developments taking place in machineries and methods of textile chemical processing. This course deals with the eco-friendly pretreatment of textiles, developments in methods and machineries used for dyeing, printing and finishing. This course will facilitate the diploma engineer to apply the knowledge related to advancement in textile processing for processing the textile and produce desired effects and finishes. It will also help the diploma engineers to analyze and rectify broad based problems in the textile chemical processing industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Produce different textiles for relevant applications using advanced textile processing.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select bio-technology based auxiliaries in pretreatment processes.
  • CO2 - Use new generation dyes, dyeing techniques and machineries for fabric processing
  • CO3 - Use recent developments in printing techniques and machineries for fabric processing
  • CO4 - Use recent developments in finishing techniques and finishing machineries for fabric processing
  • CO5 - Select relevant Nano or coating technology based on end uses in textile wet processing.
