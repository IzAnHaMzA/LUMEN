I. RATIONALE

Basic relevant knowledge of physical and organic chemistry is essential for understanding various processes in diploma in Textile Technology Programme. One such process titled as "textile wet processing" is based on the use of organic and inorganic chemicals for the wet treatment of textiles. The prerequisite knowledge of solution preparation and handling of chemicals is essential while applying the chemical on a substrate. Basic knowledge of physical chemistry is also required for the preparation of solutions of different concentrations, dye liquor, and printing paste. Some textile auxiliaries and fibers are organic in nature. Therefore, the knowledge of organic chemistry is essential for understanding textile processes like scouring, bleaching, dyeing, printing, and finishing. The course contents are designed in such a way that will ultimately help the diploma students of the Textile technology Programme to understand the concepts in a better way.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply principles of physical and organic chemistry to select relevant chemicals for solving textile processing-related problems.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Optimize textile processes based on measurement of concentration and pH of textile auxiliaries
  • CO2 - Prepare solutions of accurate concentrations required in textiles
  • CO3 - Choose relevant oxidizing and reducing agents for bleaching, dyeing and printing.
  • CO4 - Use relevant organic compounds and reaction in the textile industry.
  • CO5 - Use Aliphatic and Aromatic Compounds in Textile Technology.
  • CO6 - Select relevant extraction method for separation of organic compounds.
