I. RATIONALE

Industry always expect to have certain basic skills of operation and performing tasks of machine and tools. This equally applicable for Textile industry textile manufacturing and fashion/ clothing. It is also necessary to have preliminary skills in operating related equipment in different types of workshops. This course renders to be a pre-requisite course for courses ahead. It also provides the basics in handling equipment, machines used in Textile manufacturing, processing and garment manufacturing industry. This course is aimed to develop core hands-on skills related to work as well as use of fire fighting equipment from safety point of view, which are required in the textile and garment industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the industry expected outcome through various teaching learning experiences: Maintain machines, tools and fire safety equipment used in Textile/ Fashion and Clothing industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use relevant tools and gauges employed in Textile manufacturing and processing industry.
  • CO2 - Use appropriate tools, gauges and machines of Fashion and clothing industry
  • CO3 - Print fabric by block / screen printing.
  • CO4 - Identify use of 3-D printing, laser cutting, CNC router machines.
  • CO5 - Perform mock-drill related to fire fighting in simulated environment.
