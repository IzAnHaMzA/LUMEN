I. RATIONALE

In textile industries, textiles are manufactured using varieties of fibres or substrates such as natural, regenerated, modified regenerated as well as synthetic substrates. In textile industries, diploma engineers are expected to deal with these substrates in chemical processing of textiles. They must posses the relevant knowledge and skills of substrates processing, their chemical compositions, chemical structures, physical as well as chemical properties, and application areas. This course will help the diploma engineers to apply the basic concepts of synthetic substrates to solve broad problems in the textile industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use relevant regenerated, modified regenerated and synthetic substrates for textile chemical processing.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply relevant spinning method to manufacture Regenerated, Modified Regenerated and Synthetic substrates.
  • CO2 - Use relevant chemical process for Regenerated and Modified regenerated substrates to suite substrates properties.
  • CO3 - Use relevant chemical process for Polyester and Nylon fibre to suite fibre properties
  • CO4 - Use relevant chemical process for Acrylic fibres to suite fibre properties
  • CO5 - Use relevant chemical process for Industrial fibres to suite fibre properties
