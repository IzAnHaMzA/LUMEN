I. RATIONALE

A diploma holder in an agricultural engineering discipline is expected to design and draw simple machine components used in agriculture industries. With perquisites knowledge of Applied Mechanics, Strength of Materials, Engineering Materials, Engineering Drawing is essential.Agricultural machinery plays a crucial role in modern farming practices, increasing efficiency and productivity.Overall, the integration of Theory of machine and design in agricultural engineering contributes to the advancement of farming practices, ensuring the sustainability,safety, and economic viability of agricultural operations.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Enhance agricultural competitiveness through optimized Theory of Machine and Design application.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify various links and select suitable mechanisms for given application
  • CO2 - Interpret the motion of cam and followers
  • CO3 - Recommend relevant belts, chains and drives for the different applications.
  • CO4 - Select appropriate materials and aesthetics for given machine Components
  • CO5 - Design of joints, power transmission elements and springs for given conditions

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Assignment
  • Draw a neat sketch of ‘Locomotive coupler’ mechanism? Explain its working in brief
  • Draw the following displacement diagram for follower: (i) S.H.M (ii) Uniform acceleration and retardation
  • Give the classification of kinematic pairs. Explain any one
  • State the advantages of roller follower over knife edge follower
  • Differentiate between mechanism and machine
  • Give classification of followers and explain any one
  • Write down general machine design procedure in detail
  • Define term ‘factor of safety’. State its importance in design of machine elements
  • Define the following terms with reference to spring: i)Solid Length, ii) Free Length, iii) Spring index, iv) Spring rate, v) Pitch
  • State the effect of keyway on the strength of the shaft
  • List the factors to be considered while selecting the material for design of Machine element
  • Explain with suitable example the importance of Aesthetics while designing Machine elements
    • Micro project
  • Prepare different types of springs used in bike, bicycle,auto rikshaw,Moped and write their specifications and basis for selection
  • Prepare working model of any one mechanism using low cost materials
  • Prepare animations of various mechanisms using free software’s available on internet
  • Market survey of belts for collecting their specifications
  • Market survey to collect information about applications of timing belts
  • Take any day to day life component, find load, stresses and also prepare chart/model for the same
  • Make models of various joints and highlight resisting sections of different elements
  • Make models of various shafts, keys, coupling and highlight resisting sections
