I. RATIONALE

Every engineering structure such as dams, earthen Structure etc. are supported by soil and rock the stability of these structures depends upon behaviors of soil and capacity of soil to carry loads under different environmental conditions. The soil and rocks are also used as construction materials for embankments, roads, dams, mud walls etc. Thus, it becomes mandatory to learn this subject which includes knowledge of physical properties, classification of soil, it's behavior and various techniques to improve soil properties as well as agricultural structures i.e. farm stead's, poultry houses, dairy barns, water supply system and sanitations, grain storage structures and silos

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Suggest appropriate soil properties for agricultural use and recommend suitable soils for agricultural structures.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify types of soil strata.
  • CO2 - Interpret soil as three phase system and establish relationships between soil properties
  • CO3 - Compute properties of soil by standard test and plot particle size distribution curves.
  • CO4 - Apply Rankine’s theory to determine earth pressure in cohesive soils.
  • CO5 - Compute optimum values for the moisture content for Maximum Dry Density of Soil.
