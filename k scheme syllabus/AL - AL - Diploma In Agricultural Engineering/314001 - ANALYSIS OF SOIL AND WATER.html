I. RATIONALE

Soil and water are essential for the proper growth and nourishment of plants. Soil testing is an efficient tool for determination of soil fertility status and to access the nutrient requirement of the crops. Water is a universal solvent. It contains variable quantities of dissolved solids and gases. Sometimes, suspended and colloidal, organic and inorganic material occurs as well. Water is also essential for the germination of seeds, growth of plant roots and nutrition. The students will learn the various water testing methods, its quality and types.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply latest techniques and methods to analysis of soil and water to Prepare Soil Maps and Fertilizer recommendation using Soil and Water Test Reports.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify chemical elements of soil and evaluate soil forming processes, composition of soil
  • CO2 - Identify need of soil microbiology and understand biochemical activities
  • CO3 - Suggest appropriate standards for soil quality
  • CO4 - Suggest appropriate water quality parameters and water chemistry
  • CO5 - Select specific water treatment technology for water management.
