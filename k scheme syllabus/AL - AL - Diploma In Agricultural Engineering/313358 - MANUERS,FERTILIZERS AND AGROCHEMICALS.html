I. RATIONALE

Soil is one of the most important natural resource. soil fertility and its health declining day by day. This course develop skills of manures, fertilizer, agrochemical application and their role in sustainable agriculture. Interested students can open Agri clinic and Agribusiness centers as there are lots of opportunities in industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Plan and use of manure, fertilizers and agrochemicals for increasing the agricultural yield.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Suggest appropriate manures for given soil.
  • CO2 - Plan organic manure for a farm.
  • CO3 - Select appropriate fertilizers for given soil.
  • CO4 - Apply appropriate fertilizers for given soil as per schedule and doses.
  • CO5 - Use appropriate agrochemicals for crops.
