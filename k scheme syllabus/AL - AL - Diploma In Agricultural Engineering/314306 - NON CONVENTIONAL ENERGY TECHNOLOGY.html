I. RATIONALE

Use of renewable resources of energy is the need of the hour. Solar, Wind, Micro-Hydro and Bio-fuel systems have impacted on day to day life now and their share is increasingly capturing the eyes of researchers and stakeholders. India has set high targets of employing renewable sources of energy (Like GOI is promoting the use through advertising of MNRE Ministry of Renewable Energy Resources) for all possible applications to reduce the dependency on the fossil fuels. The requirement of trained experts for installation, operation and maintenance of various systems and equipment's Solar, wind, micro-hydro and bio-fuel systems is increasing day by day. The self employability in this course has immense potential. This course is useful for agriculture engineering in Solar, Wind, Micro-hydro and bio-fuel systems

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Prepare, Plans, Contour and projects for Non Conventional Energy.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Interpret various non-conventional energy resources and impact of solar energy.
  • CO2 - Interpret parametric study on wind energy resources.
  • CO3 - Implement micro hydro power systems and fuel cells for different applications.
  • CO4 - Identify different bio-energy resources, generation methods and technologies.
  • CO5 - Implement feasibility studies of renewable energy hybrid systems.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Micro projects on Preparing feasibility report and present it in the PPT format (Group of max 3 students) for employing renewable energy system for any given situation where fossil fuels are used. Following guidelines may be followed.
    i. Visit different industrial or household situations to assess energy needs.
    ii. Estimate the annual energy requirements using surveys and questionnaires.
    iii. Choose suitable renewable energy technology based on local resource availability.
    iv. Estimate the budget for system installation using market prices and installation costs.
  • Prepare a model of solar charger/Solar car/Solar fan/Solar torch/Solar cooler/Solar street light etc.
  • Present PPT and submit a detailed report on horizontal axis wind mill or to visit a nearest wind farm.
  • Present PPT and submit a detailed report on the visited biogas plant or biomass plant.
  • Prepare the feasibility report, incorporating financial metrics like Return on Investment (ROI) and payback period.
  • Prepare small working models of already existing/improved/new Horizontal/vertical wind turbine, Flat plate collector used for domestic water heating application and used for process heating in commercial /industrial organization, Stand-alone solar photovoltaic lighting plant, Grid connected wind mill plant, Hybrid plants, Wind-Solar PV hybrid system, Smokeless Chulhas, Burners, Heaters and Engines, Bio-gas plant.
  • To prepare a Report the most suitable types of solar lighting systems based on efficiency, cost, and environmental impact for a small colony or your institute campus.
  • Visit and prepare report on a commercial or industrial solar water heating installation of at least 500 liters per day capacity.
  • Present PPT on comparison of constructional details and performance of conventional FPC and evacuated tube FPC.
  • Prepare a layout of solar water heating system for domestic/commercial use. Present PPT and submit a detailed report on it.
  • Present PPT and submit a detailed report on various types of solar dryer designs and select best suited dryer for a given application.
  • Present PPT and submit a detailed report on PV cells: Various points to be covered are classification - monocrystalline, polycrystalline, thick film, thin film, amorphous, organic energy generation mechanism, applications etc.
    • Assignment
  • Provide recommendations for Bio-Fuel installation and use. Assess performance and feasibility of hybrid energy systems.
  • Differentiate between Flat Plate, Parabolic, and Parabolic Dish Solar Collectors (Deliver PPT presentation).
  • Compare Non-Conventional Energy Technologies (Prepare detail report).
  • Investigate wind power resources and site attributes (Prepare report from MNRE schemes).
  • Evaluate the detailed of PV cell types, benefits, and limitations (Deliver PPT presentation).
  • Create awareness of various sites based on wind analysis (Prepare report from MNRE schemes).
  • Evaluate the commercial feasibility and cost-effectiveness of renewable projects.
  • Create the micro hydro systems for renewable energy (Create video and upload on Institute website and YouTube channel)
  • Undertake fuel cell principles and their uses (Deliver PPT presentation)
  • Apply & Evaluate solar collector performance and their uses (Watch NPTEL lectures and prepare report).
  • Analyze various steps for solar system installation in different applications (Prepare report from NPTEL video lecture).
