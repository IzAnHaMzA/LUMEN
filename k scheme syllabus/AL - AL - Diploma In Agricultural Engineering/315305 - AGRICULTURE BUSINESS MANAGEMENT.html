I. RATIONALE

Agribusiness management had always played a crucial role in the development and growth of the agricultural sector and agro-based industrialization. It helps to improve the productivity and efficiency of agricultural operations, which ultimately leads to increased profitability and sustainability for farmers and agribusiness firms.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Plan, implement, monitor and evaluate the agribusiness unit and financial aspects for sustainable goals.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Plan different economic activities in small agro-business units.
  • CO2 - Suggest needs of agro-based industries for efficient resource use.
  • CO3 - Apply different management functions for effective management of agro based industries.
  • CO4 - Apply the concepts of financial management in agro-industries.
  • CO5 - Suggest the marketing and distribution skills of agro products
