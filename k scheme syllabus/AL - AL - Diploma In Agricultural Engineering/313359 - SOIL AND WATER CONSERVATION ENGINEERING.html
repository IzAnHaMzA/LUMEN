I. RATIONALE

Soil & Water Conservation Engineering is the application of engineering and agronomical principles to the solution of soil & Water management problems. With perquisites of basics about soil sciences, this course is aimed to equip the learner with different skills required for taking effective measures against soil and water erosion which will help in watershed management and planning. .

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Suggest appropriate measures for conservation of soil and water.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the structure and texture of soil.
  • CO2 - Identify forms of precipitation and importance of hydrological cycle
  • CO3 - Analyze types of soil erosion, its causes, gully development stages and gully control measures
  • CO4 - Suggest appropriate soil ,water erosion control measure for the given area and estimation of soil loss
  • CO5 - Suggest water harvesting techniques for the given area
