I. RATIONALE

This course is essential for students to grasp the fundamentals of farm machinery. It covers a range of topics from tillage to harvesting, vital for understanding agricultural operations. By studying this course, students gain the ability to recognize and differentiate farm machinery types, understand their components, and develop a comprehensive understanding of agricultural equipment.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Recommend various farm machinery, equipment, components and their applications.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the Elements of Farm Mechanization
  • CO2 - Identify the Functionality of Tillage Implements
  • CO3 - Identify the working of Sowing equipments
  • CO4 - Suggest Plant Protection Equipment in Agricultural Practices
  • CO5 - Explore Essential Harvesting and Threshing Equipment

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Assignment
  • Collect the information of two advanced harvesting equipment in your area such as constructional details and working procedure. Make a report.
  • Collect the information of minimum three sprayers and dusters available in the market.
  • Prepare pictorial charts of atleast three primary and secondary tillage equipments of each.
  • Collect the information of minimum five agricultural machinery in your area.
  • Prepare detailed information regarding component of power thresher in your locality.
  • Find out the need of seed drill calibration (Make a report).
  • Enlist two different types of seed drills and its components (Student should go to library and do the assignment).
  • Prepare a chart showing different agricultural operations and its implements.
    • Micro project
  • Collect the information of minimum five types of harvesting equipment with photographs
  • Prepare chart showing different seed metering mechanisms used in seed drill and planter
  • Prepare a chart showing agricultural operations from seed bed preparation to harvest and its implements and make a presentation submit it online (email, google classroom).
  • Visit nearby farm machinery manufacturing unit and collect a information (Company, model, specification, price and repair maintenance information) and catalog for primary and secondary tillage equipment
  • Conduct a market survey about the different kinds of dusters and sprayers available in local market (Submit it online).
  • Prepare a chart of different types of threshing methods and adjustments required in thresher.
  • Collect photographs of components of Mould Board Plough, Disk Plough, Rotavator, Cultivator and compare with thier fucntion and make a detailed report (Make a group of five students).
