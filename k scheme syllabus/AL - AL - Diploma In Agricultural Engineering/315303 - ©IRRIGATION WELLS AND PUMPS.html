I. RATIONALE

This course covers the fundamentals of surface and subsurface flow, emphasizing the role of groundwater in the hydrologic cycle, the relation of groundwater flow to geologic structure, and the management of contaminated groundwater. The objective of the course is to enable the students to have knowledge of types of tube well screens and their materials. Direct rotary, reverse rotary and air rotary drills, drilling procedures and their various types. Common troubles in irrigation wells and their remedies with the rehabilitation of sick and failed wells. The course also includes installation, operation, maintenance and troubleshooting of pumps vertical turbine pumps and submersible pumps. Pumping plant design and economical pipe size selection.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Suggest the water lifting devices for irrigation of crops in the given field.

  • CO5 - Analyze the performance of water lifting devices as per the requirement.
