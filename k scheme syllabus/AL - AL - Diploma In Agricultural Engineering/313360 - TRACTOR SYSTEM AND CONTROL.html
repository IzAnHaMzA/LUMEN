I. RATIONALE

Students should have basic knowledge of tractors, which are essential for various farming tasks. The course is vital in agricultural engineering programs, specifically in Farm Power and tractor. Students would be skilled in tractor systems, power transmission, controls, and their critical components. Mastering this course provides a solid groundwork for further exploration of farm tractor and equipment in advanced studies. Interested students can start tractor garage.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Suggest the use of tractor operations for different agricultural practices.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select various farm power sources for different applications.
  • CO2 - Suggest the tractor type for the given climate and soil condition.
  • CO3 - Maintain components of fuel supply, cooling, lubrication systems of tractor.
  • CO4 - Troubleshoot problems in power transmission system of tractor.
  • CO5 - Suggest the tractor power outlets and controls for the given situation.

VII. SUGGESTED MICRO PROJECT / ASSIGNMENT/ ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIFIC LEARNING / SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (SELF LEARNING)
Micro project
  • Visit to the Nearby Tractor showrooms and collect catalogue of different types of tractors. (Compare various tractor according to Price, Horse power and prepare a report)
  • Prepare Pictorial charts of agricultural equipment working on 2-stokes and 4-strokes engines showing their applications.(Stationary engines such as; grass cutter, chaff cutter, spraying pump etc.)
  • Collect photographs of different components of fuel supply system and cooling system. Take out a print of the photographs and label the photographs. (Visit nearby Tractor workshop for the above task)
  • Conduct a market survey (Collect catalogues and information like; company, model, Specifications, price, utility, repair and maintenance, spare parts, etc.) and prepare a Flow-chart to get information of Various tractor power outlets and its application (PTO, Drawbar Horsepower, Single Hitch point). Record videos and upload on social media
  • Prepare one miniature tractor model representing working of different tractor systems (Students can use mechanical workshop available in the institute)
    • Assignment
  • Draw the Diagram of Power Train used in tractor for power transmission. (All component(with specifications) used in power transmission and Submit it online)
  • Enlist five situations wherein tractor control is essential. (Search on web and submit online)(Student should go to the library and do the assignment)
  • State various applications of hydraulic system in a tractor. (Conduct field visits, exhibitions, Showrooms, do collect the information and give detailed presentation)
  • Identify different tractor trouble shooting and prepare a report on potential cause.(Such as; Engine overheating, Engine won’t start, Hydraulic function malfunction, Transmission slipping, Electrical system issues)
  • Do a street play on emerging technology used in farm mechanization to overcome labor shortage. (Create a group of 7-8 students and perform play in the society/village or on street to spread awareness about emerging technology in agriculture sector)
  • Identify different types of tractors used in abroad for heavy task (Student should go to the library and Collect the pictures with specifications give detailed presentation.)
