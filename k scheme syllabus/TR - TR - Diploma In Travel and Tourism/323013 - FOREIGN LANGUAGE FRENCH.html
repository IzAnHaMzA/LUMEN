I. RATIONALE

Travel and Tourism students must acquire proficiency in a second worldwide language. The need for interpreters is growing as more tourists travel to India. Students who take this course will be able to hold basic conversations with foreign guests. They will be able to help visitors from other countries with hotel reservations, restaurant reservations, at the airport, travel agencies, and tourist places.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
After studying this course, students will be able to assist and guide the foreign traveler.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Learn French pronunciation.
  • CO2 - Use correct grammar while communicating with the guest.
  • CO3 - Guide the tourist with directions, time, seasons, and meals .
  • CO4 - Use different travel vocabulary while communicating with the tourists.
  • CO5 - Communicate for basic requirement with French tourist at Hotel, restaurant, Airport and Travel agency's
