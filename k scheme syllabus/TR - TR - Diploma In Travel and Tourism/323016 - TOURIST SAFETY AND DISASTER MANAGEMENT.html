I. RATIONALE

This course offers a comprehensive and strategic approach to understanding the importance of disaster planning and management in tourism. It offers a major aspect, including environmental hazards, geography, transportation accidents and catastrophes, tourist safety, and public relations. It aims to integrate these often disparate fields to foster understanding and future knowledge development in the tourism and transportation fields, allowing learners to comprehend this complex area, improve their digital capabilities, resourcefulness, and resilience, and apply their learning in future employment toward a more resilient and enhanced sustainable tourism in India and work for tourist safety during the tour.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Prepare a Plan for tourist safety and disaster management, by utilizing a variety of scholarly and professional resources.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the key aspects involved in disaster management.
  • CO2 - Classify the types of disaster their origin causes and their management and the disaster profile of India.
  • CO3 - Employ proficient theoretical and scientific knowledge in disaster management.
  • CO4 - Prepare plan for handle disaster event for tourist safety.
