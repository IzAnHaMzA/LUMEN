I. RATIONALE

This course will focus on the introduction of the front office department to the students. This course focuses on the understanding of basic concepts, organization, and the basic functions of the department. This course will help the students to better the inter and intra-departmental communication and guest cycle

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Assists in front office works.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the departments and functions of front office department.
  • CO2 - Assists in duties and responsibilities of front office department.
  • CO3 - Plan the guests life cycle stages
  • CO4 - Reserve rooms as per the guests request.
  • CO5 - Use various channels and sources of reservations.
