I. RATIONALE

Travel professionals use destination geography every day. It is essential for the same to comprehend where the numerous countries are located and how they are divided into areas. In this course, students will acquire the fundamental knowledge necessary to serve clients promptly and accurately. They will be able to calculate the travel time, understand the world time zones, recognize the continents and nations they are in, and understand IATA geography. They will be able to identify the tourist attractions of the countries in the Western Hemisphere and understand their importance in tourism. In this course, the students will learn the basic knowledge required for planning and selling tour packages of the western hemisphere countries.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: Provide information on western world tourist destinations.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify IATA World Divisions.
  • CO2 - Provide information related to tourist destinations in USA and Mexico.
  • CO3 - Provide information related to tourist destinations in Argentina, Brazil, and Peru.
  • CO4 - Provide information related to tourist destinations in Europe.
  • CO5 - Provide information related to major wildlife sanctuaries and National Parks in the continent of Africa.
