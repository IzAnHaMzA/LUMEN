I. RATIONALE

Event management includes the strategic planning, coordination, and implementation of live events, ranging from conferences to product launches to exhibitions to concerts. In light of this, event management has emerged as one of India's most promising job options. Many people find event management to be an exciting and engaging career that demands a lot of work and effort. This course helps students to develop the skills to design, plan, create, implement, and manage various events.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry-identified outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Enable the students to plan and organize events.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identfiy and classify various events.
  • CO2 - Plan a corporate and private events.
  • CO3 - Coordinate between various aspects of the event.
  • CO4 - Demonstrate qualities and skills required for event management.
  • CO5 - Manage the event project.
