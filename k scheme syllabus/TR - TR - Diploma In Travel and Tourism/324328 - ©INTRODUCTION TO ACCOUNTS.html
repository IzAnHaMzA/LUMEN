I. RATIONALE

“Accounting is like a salt and spice in food, no one appreciates precision & presence but notices absence and deficiency”. Accounting is a language of the business while bookkeeping is an art & technique. Explaining principles, procedure, using tools of accounting along with their advantages and limitations would be essential to cope up with the industry. Costing the activity and fixing selling price is a big task for tourism industry, pricing too high may frighten the traveler and too low may leads the business to losses. Taking right decisions accurately and timely is a good sign of healthy management.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry-identified outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Keep proper record using accounting tools, calculate tour cost and fix selling price.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select appropriate business form and recording method.
  • CO2 - Practice accounting principles and apply GST on transactional value.
  • CO3 - Prepare ledger, interpret balance and draft Cash Book.
  • CO4 - Calculate tour cost and quote tour price.
  • CO5 - Select appropriate business composition to ensure growth.
