I. RATIONALE

“Create need to generate demand”, Marketing is a backbone of any business. Effective marketing ensures better chances of survival in the current era of cut-throat competition. Understanding tourists as consumer and market behaviour would be essential to correlate Segmentation-Targeting-Positioning. Marketing Research, forecasting of demand and sales, framing appropriate and timely plan about the product, price, promotion and distribution activities is an essential part of successful marketing. Satisfaction of consumer for Revenue generation and Retention is a big task for Tourism industry as of transient nature of tourists.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Locate, target tourist and complete selling process using marketing mix for revenue generation and guest satisfaction.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use Service Marketing Mix.
  • CO2 - Correlate segment with consumer behaviour for positioning the product.
  • CO3 - Implement Pricing plans for tourism product.
  • CO4 - Carry out digital marketing for Promotion & Distribution.
  • CO5 - Execute selling process.
