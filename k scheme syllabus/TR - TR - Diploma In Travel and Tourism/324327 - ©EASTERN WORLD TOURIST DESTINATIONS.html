I. RATIONALE

Travel professionals use destination geography every day. It is essential for them to comprehend where the numerous countries are located and how they are divided into areas. In this course, students will acquire the fundamental knowledge necessary to serve clients promptly and accurately. They will be able to identify the tourist attractions of the countries in the Eastern Hemisphere and understand their importance in tourism. In this course, the students will learn the basic knowledge required for planning and selling tour packages of the Eastern hemisphere countries.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: Provide information on Eastern world tourist destinations.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Explore information related to tourist destinations in the Middle East.
  • CO2 - Disseminate information related to tourist destinations in South Asia.
  • CO3 - Search information related to tourist destinations in Southeast Asia.
  • CO4 - Locate information related to tourist destinations in Far East Asia.
  • CO5 - Explore information related to tourist destinations in Australia continent.
