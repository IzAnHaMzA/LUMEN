I. RATIONALE

Numerous travel agencies rely on tour escorts to guide group tours, with the tour manager assuming the leadership role for the group. This program aims to emphasize the essential skills necessary for effective tour escorting and the intricacies involved in leading a tour. Participants will gain insight into the pivotal role and significance of tour escorts within the travel industry.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help the student attain the following industry-identified outcomes through various teaching-learning experiences: Escort and manage a tour effectively.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Prepare job roles of a guide and an escort.
  • CO2 - Cordinate with travel agent .
  • CO3 - Coordinate with customer and supplier.
  • CO4 - Handle special situations during tour.
  • CO5 - Follow gender and age sensitive service practices throughout the duration of the tour.

 Unit - III Functions of Tour Escort
3.1 Function before departure of tour: coordination with customer and travel agent, confirmation of all services such as hotel booking, tickets, travel document passenger details- Valid visa & passport details of travelers, foreign currency (For world Tour) , travel insurance, supplier details, tour package includes and excludes, tour Itinerary with local sightseeing timings with guide, entry tickets booking and other services offer by travel agent for tourist.
3.2 Function during the tour: Receive customer with a smile or welcoming coordinate with driver for sightseeing and transfers, coordinate with hotel for accommodation ,food and other special event, local sightseeing planning as per tour itinerary and coordination with driver and tour guide. Instruction to travelers regarding schedule, safety of tourist and local rules at destination, resolve the problems during the tour, provide feedback to reporting officers..
3.3 Function post tour: Collection of feedback, suggest Implementation in the up coming tour. preparation of planning to deal with unsatisfied customer. analysis of customers reviews.
