I. RATIONALE

Pleasing personality, well-groomed outlook and a pleasant smile are the requirements of a travel professional. The students after studying this subject will know that good service begins with an understanding of their role. The travel agent’s success is based upon selling an abstract product i.e. service. The course provides an opportunity to develop skills of good customer service required at the travel counter.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course is designed to guide students in achieving industry-recognized objectives via diverse teaching and learning approaches. The focus is on showcasing soft skills tailored to the specific requirements of the tourism sector.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply the technique of providing effective customer service.
  • CO2 - Identify the needs of different customers.
  • CO3 - Identify the attributes required for good customer service.
  • CO4 - Use the interpersonal and leadership skills at workplace.
  • CO5 - Use effectively various communication skills to handle customer.
