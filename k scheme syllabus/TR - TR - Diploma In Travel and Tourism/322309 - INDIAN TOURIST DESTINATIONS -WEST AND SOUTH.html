I. RATIONALE

Product knowledge is essential for planning a tour. This course intends to make the students familiar with the location of major tourist places and the best time to visit West and South India. They will relate the wide variety of tourist attractions in the form of hill stations, monuments, beaches, temples, wildlife sanctuaries, etc. This course will develop skills in the students to handle queries about domestic tourism. This will prepare them to offer essential information to the tourists as well as planning tour itineraries.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student attain the following industry-identified outcomes through various teaching-learning experiences: Plan tour routes for various places of tourist importance in West and South India.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Locate the different tourist places of Maharashtra and Goa for planning a tour.
  • CO2 - Locate the different tourist places of Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh for planning a tour.
  • CO3 - Locate and state the importance of different tourist places of Karnataka and Kerala.
  • CO4 - Locate and state the importance of different tourist places in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu and Puducherry.
  • CO5 - Examine the tourism potential of National Parks and Wild Life Sanctuaries of West and South India.
