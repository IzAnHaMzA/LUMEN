I. RATIONALE

This course intends to make students familiar with the location and Geography of tourist places, to know the best time to visit various places of tourist interest in North, North East, and East Indian states. They will also appreciate the wide variety of tourist attractions in the form of culture, tradition, wildlife sanctuaries, parks, hill stations, Beaches, Monuments, Temples, Forts, Caves, etc. However, knowledge of the destination will be useful during the planning of tour itineraries and handling of tourist queries. Furthermore, product knowledge is essential for planning of tour, India is one of the oldest civilizations in the world with a kaleidoscopic variety and rich cultural heritage. India offers a wide variety of landscapes, and a wide range of flora and wildlife. Because of its beautiful landscapes, magnificent waterfalls, habitat for the nation's largest tiger reserve, and, the country is the most popular tourist destination for visitors from all over the world. Moreover, this course prepare students to hadaling tourist queries such as how to go, what to see and when to go at tourist places.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry-identified outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Plan tour routes for various places of tourist importance in North, North East, and East India.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Analyze the physical and political location of Indian Places of Tourist Interest.
  • CO2 - Use weather and climate in the planning of the tour.
  • CO3 - Locate the different places in North India for planning a tour
  • CO4 - Locate and state the importance of different places in East India
  • CO5 - Locate and state the importance of different places in North East India
  • CO6 - Plan tour route for North, North East and East India
