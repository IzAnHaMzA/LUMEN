I. RATIONALE

This course will focus on the steps involved in establishing a travel agency. Operating a competent and effective travel agency demands a diverse set of qualifications and talents. This course focuses on managerial abilities and the implementation of various management principles and functions in business. The role and function of a travel agency are important as they have to design the travel plans for business and leisure travel which many times is complex and complicated. This course will highlight the procedure for setting up a travel agency and focuses on the managerial skills & application of various concepts and functions of management in travel business.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Establish a travel agency in future
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the travel agent, tour operator and list the qualities of a travel personnel.
  • CO2 - Offer travel related services to customer.
  • CO3 - Establish a simulated travel agency and delineate its operational structure at both organizational and departmental levels.
  • CO4 - Plan sources of income for travel agency.
  • CO5 - Plan the infrastructure set up required for starting a travel agency
