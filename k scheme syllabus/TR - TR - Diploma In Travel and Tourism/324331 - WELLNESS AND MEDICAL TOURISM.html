I. RATIONALE

Medical and Wellness tourism is one of the most rapidly emerging sectors in the world. Medical travelers travel across the globe to get the best and most effective medical treatments. India has a good status in the world for cost-effective medical treatments. In the future, there will be a high demand for labor in medical tourism. The course is intended to provide students with basic knowledge of the emerging segment of tourism with a lot of potential in India and provide a skill to get immediate placement in the sector.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Promote and develop a travel product specially for the Medical and wellness tourism industry.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Structure the dimensions of health tourism.
  • CO2 - Estimate the growth of medical tourism.
  • CO3 - Promote the wellness tourism on different factors.
  • CO4 - Utilities the services of medical and wellness packages providers.
  • CO5 - Comply with ethical and legal procedures related to medical tourism and wellness tourism in India.
