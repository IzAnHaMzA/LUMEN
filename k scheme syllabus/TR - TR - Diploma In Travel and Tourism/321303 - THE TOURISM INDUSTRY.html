I. RATIONALE

Tourism is one of the world’s largest economic sectors that creates jobs, drives exports, and generates prosperity across the world. It also has the major potential to contribute to the local economy. India being diverse in landscapes, cuisines, heritage, adventure, wildlife and culture is emerged as a well-known destination for both overseas and domestic travelers in recent years. The tourism industry is a rapidly growing in India and it is the most job-providing sector worldwide. As per the World Travel and Tourism Council, in 2019, Travel and Tourism was one of the world’s largest sectors, accounting for 10.4% of global GDP (USD 9.2 trillion), 10.6% of all jobs (334 million), and was responsible for creating 1 in 4 of all new jobs across the world. This course will introduce the students to significant aspects of the tourism industry. The student will realize that a single trip involves many others who work behind the scenes. They will get familiarized with the constituents of Travel and Tourism and will be aware of the vast career opportunities available in this field.

  • CO3 - Integrate tourism infrastructure in tourism products and services.
  • CO4 - Evaluate the impact of tourism industry on various aspects of socio-economic development employment.
  • CO5 - Use the different types of motivations for planning the tour.
  • CO6 - Explore career opportunities and options to work as Travel agent and Tour Operator.
