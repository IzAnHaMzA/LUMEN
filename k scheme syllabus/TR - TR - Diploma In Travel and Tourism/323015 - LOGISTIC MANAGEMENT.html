I. RATIONALE

Logistics Management is part of supply chain management. It plans, implements, and controls the efficient, effective forward and reverse flow and storage of goods, services, and related information between the point of origin and the point of consumption to meet customers’ requirements. This course provides practical and management perspective on cargo operations.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help the student attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: *Handle the cargo operations related to the booking.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Use the logistic Management framework.
  • CO2 - Select the applicable cargo rate.
  • CO3 - Document for Cargo handling.
  • CO4 - Prepare a plan for risk management.
