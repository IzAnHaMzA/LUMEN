I. RATIONALE

This course aims to familiarize students with management principles, functions, and abilities with a focus on how they apply to the tourism industry. This course also emphasizes managerial skills, the application of various management concepts in this industry, and tour operation management.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Operate and manage a tour in a tour Operator Company.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply the concept and meaning of Management.
  • CO2 - Draft Planning and decision-making process.
  • CO3 - Organize a work and handle the manpower.
  • CO4 - Operate a tour.
  • CO5 - Set up a travel agency.
