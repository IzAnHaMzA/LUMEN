I. RATIONALE

Hospitality is an essential part of the tourism industry and one of the major stakeholders of the tourism industry. It is contributing in a big way for the development of the tourism industry in India and throughout the globe. The knowledge about this industry can help promotion of the tourism sector in the forthcoming era. This course intends to develop skills required in various departments of the hospitality industry. Students will also be able to grade, the wide variety of hotels, categories of hotel, amenities and different types of services offered by the hotel as well the role and responsibilities of the various employees in the hotel.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: Co-ordinate with hotel while planning and during the tour.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Infer history and present status of hotel Industry.
  • CO2 - Coordinate with different department in hotel during the tour .
  • CO3 - Categorise room types based on services and amenities.
  • CO4 - Recommend meals plan to the customers while selecting packages.
  • CO5 - Use hotel terminology.
