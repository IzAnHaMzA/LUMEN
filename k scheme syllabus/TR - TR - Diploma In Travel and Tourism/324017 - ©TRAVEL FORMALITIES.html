I. RATIONALE

Travelers go across regional, state, and international borders as part of tourism. Before departing from one country and entering another, travelers must fulfill certain requirements. After completing this course students should be able to apply for a passport and understand the requirements for different kinds of visas for different countries. This course will cover all the necessary processes that a traveler must fulfill to ensure that their journey is free of obstructions and does not pose a threat to the country they are visiting.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Apply for online passports, Visas, and various permissions for tourists.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply passport online for different age category.
  • CO2 - Prepare a document file to apply visa online.
  • CO3 - Identify the health prevention measures for travelers and advice travel insurance.
  • CO4 - Guide on custom regulation for international passenger.
  • CO5 - Identify the Airport formalities.
