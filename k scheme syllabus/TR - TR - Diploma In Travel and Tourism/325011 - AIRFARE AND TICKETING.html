I. RATIONALE

This course covers the fundamentals of airfare construction and general ticketing regulations, making it a crucial component of the tourism business. The course also focuses on applying various concepts to calculate different fares. Students will be able to get best fare on GDS and advice the best routing to customers by international airline agreements. The Students will be able to make online reservations for flights and guide passengers about customs and baggage rules while flying on air.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Construct different airfares as per the demand of passengers.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the IATA areas and subareas as well as the frequently used airline codes.
  • CO2 - Construct fare and issue the tickets on GDS.
  • CO3 - Use the Airline terminology while air ticket reservation and communication.
  • CO4 - Comply with the international air transport regulation and online reservation procedures.
  • CO5 - Consult baggage for specific airlines and design in-flight services for different classes of airlines.
