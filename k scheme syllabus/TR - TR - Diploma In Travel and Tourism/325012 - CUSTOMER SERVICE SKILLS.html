I. RATIONALE

Pleasing personality, well-groomed outlook and a pleasant smile are the requirements of a travel professional. Travellers have a wide range of options of various services. Excellent customer service is often the difference between choosing you or your competitor. Clear and effective communication is the foundation of excellent customer service. Tourists nowadays are internet savvy and can book their own holidays while checking the best reviews. The travel agent’s success is based upon selling an intangible product i.e., service. The course provides an opportunity to develop Effective customer handling techniques and how to provide exceptional customer service. Tourism being service industry applying knowledge of customer service skills play vital role in preparing students industry ready.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
The aim of this course is to help the student to attain the following industry-identified outcome through various teaching-learning experiences: Incorporate developed Customer service skills while offering services to the tourists
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Deploy Customer Service and Work Ethics in the Tourism Industry.
  • CO2 - Identify the attributes required for Effective Customer Handling.
  • CO3 - Design plans to build Customer Loyalty.
  • CO4 - Use Technology in Serving Customers.
  • CO5 - Apply various steps to Calm the Customer.
