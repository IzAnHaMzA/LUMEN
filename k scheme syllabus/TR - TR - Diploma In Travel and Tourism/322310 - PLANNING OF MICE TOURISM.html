I. RATIONALE

Business travel and the GDP of the nations are strongly and comparatively correlated. For the benefit of the national economy, business tourism can yield a variety of financial benefits. Business travel is a major source of employment for individuals in many nations. In contrast to leisure tourists, business travelers have higher demands for travel-related services, which makes MICE a significant component of business tourism. India is a developing nation that has concentrated on building MICE infrastructure; examples of these include Yashobhumi and Bharat Mandapam. The Meeting, Incentives, Conventions, and Exhibitions (MICE) industry is the focus of this course, which builds abilities in this area.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
This course aims to help the student attain the following industry-identified competency through various teaching-learning experiences: * Plan and execute MICE tourism activities
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Identify the requirements of a business traveler
  • CO2 - Compute the potential of MICE tourism in India
  • CO3 - Organise the various types of meeting
  • CO4 - Plan a conference and other related events
  • CO5 - Organise conferences/Exhibitions/ Incentive tours
