I. RATIONALE

Tour Package is an important function of Travel agents and Tour operators. In this course, students will realize the importance of the Tour Package and the information required to plan a good tour plan. They will come to know the procedure to calculate the cost of the tours and develop different tour packages as per the needs of the customer.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Prepare different tour packages as per the demand of the customer
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Select suitable components of Tour Package.
  • CO2 - Select travel Itinerary as per the requirement and its types
  • CO3 - Prepare travel itineraries for different destinations.
  • CO4 - Design a tour packages for different destinations and customers.
  • CO5 - Calculate a tour package cost.
