I. RATIONALE

The course helps in understanding the resource potential for adventure tourism in India and the existing popular destinations and activities. It also explains the major constraints on way to adventure tourism development and the possible strategies to overcome the same. It enables students to prepare itineraries and execute adventure tours.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Prepare adventure tour itineraries and execute adventure tours.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Interpret the meaning and potential of adventure tourism in India.
  • CO2 - Inform the tourists about the equipment, preferred destinations, fitness and energy level required for land based adventure tourism activities.
  • CO3 - Inform the tourists about the equipment, preferred destinations, fitness and energy level required for water based adventure tourism activities.
  • CO4 - Inform the tourists about the equipment, preferred destinations, fitness and energy level required for air based tourism activities.
