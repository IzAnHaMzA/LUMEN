I. RATIONALE

Machining success hinges significantly on the quality of the tools employed. Optimal tool selection, considering factors such as shape, size, and material is paramount for achieving efficient and top-notch machining results. Employing jigs and fixtures facilitates swift and secure tool positioning, thereby enhancing machining outcomes. This course empowers students with the expertise to choose the best-suited tools for diverse machining assignments. Furthermore, it comprehensively covers the design aspects of cutting tools, jigs, and fixtures, imparting essential knowledge about these fundamental elements of machining.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Use different tools, dies, jigs and fixtures as per the requirement.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Evaluate cutting tool geometry and its tool signatures.
  • CO2 - Apply locating and clamping concept to a given component.
  • CO3 - Design a jig and fixture for a given component.
  • CO4 - Analyze the press tool operation required for a specific component.
  • CO5 - Compute parameters of bending and drawing dies.
