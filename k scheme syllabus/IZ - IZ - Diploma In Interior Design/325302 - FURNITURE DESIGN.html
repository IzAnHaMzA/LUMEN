I. RATIONALE

Furniture design is a specialized subject within the field of architecture and interior design that focuses on the conceptualization, development, and creation of furniture pieces. This course aims to equip students with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills essential for designing furniture that is functional, aesthetically pleasing, and responsive to contemporary design trends and user needs.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Design compelling, user-centered furniture solutions integrating sustainable practices and contemporary design trends.
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Explain evolution and cultural influence of ancient furniture styles on modern design trends.
  • CO2 - Apply anthropometric principles for ergonomic comfort within the spaces.
  • CO3 - Apply principles of design to create innovative and functional furniture pieces.
  • CO4 - Prepare detailed working drawings for given furniture pieces.
  • CO5 - Explain joints and joinery techniques to construct furniture pieces.
