I. RATIONALE

Nutrition and Dietetics deals with the knowledge of healthy dietary patterns and the relationship between dietary practices and various disease states.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Evaluate relationship between nutrition and dietetics in health and/or illness
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Interpret role of nutrition in human health
  • CO2 - Assess nutritional status of food
  • CO3 - Select diet for healthy life
  • CO4 - Manage diet in diseases related to stomach, liver and kidney
  • CO5 - Suggest diet in diabetes mellitus
