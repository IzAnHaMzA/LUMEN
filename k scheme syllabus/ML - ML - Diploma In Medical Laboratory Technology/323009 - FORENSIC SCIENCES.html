I. RATIONALE

Forensic Science deals crime scene analysis.

II. INDUSTRY / EMPLOYER EXPECTED OUTCOME
Evaluate relationship between forensic Science and Crime
III. COURSE LEVEL LEARNING OUTCOMES (COS)
Students will be able to achieve & demonstrate the following COs on completion of course based learning
  • CO1 - Apply the Principle of Forensic Science in investigation.
  • CO2 - Perform Crime Scene Processing Procedure.
  • CO3 - Assess basic chemistry of toxicology.
  • CO4 - Detect common adulterant used in food product.
  • CO5 - Relate the working and function of specified Forensic science laboratory to its intended requirement.
